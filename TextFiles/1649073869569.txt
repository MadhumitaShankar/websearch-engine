Master of Business Administration or MBA is one of the most sought after courses offered at the PG level of education in India as well as abroad. The course is typically imparted in a two-year duration. The two-year professional course acts as a stepping stone to a plethora of job opportunities. Being one of the most popular PG courses, MBA attracts aspirants from all fields be it , or Arts. Two years of an MBA course is generally divided into four semesters. However, some private institutes also offer a one year MBA diploma programme. MBA aspirants can find regular degree programmes under several specialisations, executive MBA programmes, online, MBA courses, and part-time MBA courses.

MBA Colleges in Hyderabad Eligibility Criteria
The eligibility criteria for the MBA programme may differ across institutes. However, the basic eligibility criteria for MBA is as follows:

Candidates must hold a graduation degree in any discipline or equivalent from a recognised university
Institutes offering MBA in Hyderabad might have minimum score criteria in the qualifying exam of 50 percentile or equivalent for unreserved categories. For reserved category candidates, the minimum aggregate score may have a relaxation of 5 per cent with a minimum score of 45 per cent.
Some institutes might require work experience as the eligibility criteria for
MBA admission

ROI of Top MBA Colleges in Hyderabad
Return on Investment or ROI is one of the primary parameters that help aspirants choose the best MBA college in Hyderabad. ROI is the comparison of the course fee with the average placement offered by an institute. The course fee is termed as the investment made by the student, and the average placement package at the institute is considered as the return one can expect after the investment made (as course fee).
The ROI of an institute takes into consideration the average CTC and the entire course fee.

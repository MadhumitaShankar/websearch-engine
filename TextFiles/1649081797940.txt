Why are downloads not working correctly when connecting via AirPlay or HDMI?







iOS |
Under investigation


*** November 2021 ***
We are aware of an ongoing issue where downloads won't play correctly when connected to TVs via AirPlay or HDMI. The download will play back with video but no sound.
We are still investigating this and hope to complete some work very soon which should resolve the issue.
We will update this page when have further information.

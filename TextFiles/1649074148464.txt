What is RPF Constable Exam?
Indian Railways recruits male and female Constables for Railway Protection Force (RPF) and Railway Protection Special Force (RPSF) through RPF Constable Exam. Ministry of Railways releases RPF Constable Notification to fill a large number of vacancies for different zones of Indian Railways. The exam is conducted in a centralized manner, however, recruitment is done separately for various railway zones. RPF Constable Notification for this year's exam is going to be out soon. Therefore, it is important for applicants to know all basic details about the exam.

RPF Constable Exam 2022 Highlights





Exam Name

RPF Constable 2022



Conducting Body

Ministry of Railways



Exam Level

National



Exam Frequency

Once in a year/ vacancy based



Exam Mode

Online



Exam Duration

90 minutes



Language of the Paper

Candidates can choose any of the following languages:
Hindi, English, Urdu, Tamil, Telugu, Konkani, Malayalam, Kannada, Marathi, Gujurati, Bengali, Odia, Punjabi, Assamese and Manipuri.



Exam Fees

INR 500 for the General category and INR 250 for the rest of the categories



Exam Purpose

To recruit male and female constables for RPF and RPSF



Exam Website

https://indianrailways.gov.in/

RPF Constable 2022 FAQs

Q: When will RPF Constable 2022 notification be released?
A: The RPF Constable 2022 notification will be released soon.

Q: How do I apply for RPF 2022?
A: The candidates can apply for the RPF Constable 2022 exam online at the web address provided in the official notification. The candidates can see here the steps to .
apply online for RPF Constable 2022

Q: What is the age limit required for the RPF Constable Exam?
A: The age of the candidates must be between 18 and 25 years.

Q: What is the minimum qualification for RPF Constable exam?
A: Candidates must pass SSLC/Matric from a recognized board, this is the minimum educational qualification to apply for the posts.

Q: What is the salary of RPF Constables?
A: Along with good job security and perks, RPF Constables are entitled to get INR 21,700 plus usual allowances such as travel allowance, accommodation, food, etc.

Q: What kind of trainings are given to the shortlisted candidates in RPF Constable Recruitment?
A: After successfully completing the preliminary selection stages, shortlisted candidates will be given physical and arm training, these are a crucial part of the entire training process.

Q: Where do RPF Constables get postings after the selection?
A: They can be posted anywhere out of the 18 Railway Zones across the country, also they are subjected to job-related transfers as needed.

Q: What is RPF Constable application fee?
A: RPF Constable application fee for General candidates is INR 500 and for the rest of the categories, it is INR 250.

Q: In how many languages RPF Constable exam is conducted?
A: RPF Constable exam is conducted in 15 languages, candidates can pick up any language of their choice in which they are comfortable.

Q: How many marks will I get for the correct answer in RPF Constable exam?
A: For the correct answer, candidates will score one mark.

Q: Is there any negative marking in RPF Constable?
A: Yes, there is negative marking in RPF Constable exam, 1/3 marks will get deducted for each wrong answer.

Q: What is the qualifying percentage for RPF Constable recruitment?
A: To qualify RPF constable CBT, a candidate must obtain 35% marks if the individual belongs to the General category while it is 30% for SC and ST candidates.

The National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT) conducts the NIFT entrance exam to shortlist aspirants for admission to its programs such as Bachelor of Design (BDes), Bachelor of Fashion Technology (BFTech), Master of Design (MDes), Master of Fashion Management (MFM) and Master of Fashion Technology (MFTech). NIFT entrance exam is three-tiered in its format for BDes and MDes course admissions. As part of the NIFT entrance exam 2022 for BDes and MDes admission, candidates have to first appear for Creative Ability Test (CAT) and General Ability Test (GAT).

NIFT Situation Test/Group Discussion and Personal Interview 2022
The situation test for the B.Des program is being conducted from April 2-5 in offline mode. Candidates who have qualified in the NIFT 2022 written tests (GAT and CAT) and have participated in the choice filling process are eligible to participate in the situation test. Choice fillling was conducted from March 14-31, 2022. As part of the choice filling process, the candidate has to select their choice of NIFT 2022 participating institutes in the order of priority.
The final result of NIFT 2022 would be prepared by considering the Situation Test and written test scores. The weightage for each exam is given below:

CAT - 50% weightage
GAT - 30% weightage
Situation Test - 20% weightage.
The National Institute of Fashion Technology will conduct a Group Discussion and Personal Interview for the Master's programs from April 7-27. NIFT 2022 Admission Process & Selection Criteria
NIFT is popular all over the country for its design, management, and technology programmes on offer at its 17 centres. Candidates are eligible to secure admission at NIFT on the basis of the ranks they secure in the entrance test. The steps that candidates need to follow in order to secure admission in any design course offered at the 17 NIFT campuses are mentioned below.




NIFT Selection Process 2022


Step 1: Fill NIFT Application Form 2022
Candidates need to fill online on the official website of the design school. Candidates need to pay their application fee by credit/debit card or the same can be paid by means of a Demand Draft (DD). Applicants are advised to keep a printout/ photocopy of their NIFT Entrance Exam application form for reference along with proof of payment.
NIFT Entrance Exam application form

Step 2: Download NIFT Admit Card
After duly filling the application forms, candidates need to download their from the official website of NIFT. It may be noted that candidates have to download their admit card from the official website only and no hard copy of the hall ticket will be sent to them by Post.
NIFT Entrance Exam admit card

Step 3: Appear for NIFT Entrance Exam 2022
As part of the NIFT Entrance Test, candidates are evaluated on different parameters such as aptitude, skills, and knowledge. Due to this, the NIFT entrance exam is divided into four sections – Creative Ability Test (CAT), General Ability Test (GAT), Situation Test, and Group Discussion/ Personal Interview. Candidates can view the and here to know more details about how questions in the design entrance exam are structured.
NIFT exam pattern
syllabus

Step 4: Appear for NIFT Situation Test or GD/PI
Candidates who clear the NIFT entrance exam for BDes course admission then need to appear for the Situation Test round wherein their material handling skills are tested. On the other hand, aspirants taking the design entrance exam for MDes course admission need to appear for the Group Discussion/Personal Interview round. The situation test/ GD/ PI admission round is usually conducted in April-May every year.

Step 5: Check NIFT Entrance Exam Result
The final NIFT results are displayed on the official website of the design college. Candidates can view their results for NIFT admission by filling their roll number on the official website. Final results for NIFT admissions are usually declared in the month of May/June, every year.

Step 6: Appear for NIFT Counselling
Candidates are called for the NIFT counselling process as per their rank in NIFT entrance exam 2022. A separate counselling process is conducted for different design course admissions. NIFT authorities have a centralised counselling process for NIFT admission to different design programmes.
NIFT Selection Criteria 2022

For, candidates have to take the GAT exam only whereas for candidates have to appear for GAT and GD/PI admission round. FAQs on NIFT Admissions


Q. When will the NIFT 2022 GD/PI conducted for the MFM program?
A. The group discussion and personal interview for the MFM program will be conducted on April 7, 8, 11, 12, 13, 18, and 19.

Q. When will the Situation Test for the B.Des course be conducted for NIFT 2022 qualified candidates?


A. The Situation Test for the B.Des program will be conducted from April 2-5. Registration for participation in the situation test is open till March 11.

Q. When will the Group Discussion/ Personal Interview (GD/PI) be conducted for the MFM, M.Des, and MFT courses?


A. The GD/PI will be conducted from April 7-26. Candidates who have been shortlisted need to visit niftadmissions.in and get registered and upload documents for participation in the GD/PI.

Q. In what mode will the NIFT 2022 exam be conducted?


A. The NIFT 2022 exam will be conducted in online, remote-proctored mode.

Q. When will the NIFT 2022 answer key be released?


A. The National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT) has released the NIFT 2022 answer key on February 21, 2022.

Q. Will the NIFT answer key 2022 released for both CAT and GAT?


A. The NIFT 2022 answer key has only been released for the GAT written test. The link to download the answer key is given on this page.

Q. When will NIFT results 2022 be declared?


A. The result for written test for BDes, MDes, MFTech and MFM courses would be declared on March 9, 2022. The final NIFT result for all courses (except BFTech) is announced in June. The final NIFT Entrance Exam result for BFTech is usually announced in April/May every year.

Q. How can I check NIFT exam results 2022?


A. Aspirants can view NIFT result 2022 for BDes, BFTech, MDes, MFM and MFTech course admission on the official website of the design school ().

Q. What is the syllabus for NIFT entrance exam?


A. For BDes and MDes course admissions, candidates need to appear for CAT and GAT. NIFT Syllabus for CAT is such that candidates need to attempt drawing questions in the paper. Some topics that candidates should practice while preparing for the exam are design theory, creative thinking, and writing, elements and principles of design, principles of composition, etc. For GAT, candidates should study topics such as quantitative ability, English comprehension, logical reasoning, etc.

Q. What precautions were taken by authorities to conduct NIFT Entrance Exam 2021?


A. NIFT ensured that all NIFT exam centres
followed social distancing guidelines and temperature of each student was checked before entering the exam hall. Along with that, it was mandatory for candidates to carry COVID-19 self-declaration along with their admit card for NIFT Entrance Exam. Any candidate who was not carrying this document was not allowed to take the NIFT exam.

Q. Can I get direct admission to NIFT?


A. Candidates can secure admission directly in the third semester of NIFT UG courses. This is called 'Lateral Entry'. NIFT lateral entry admission (NLEA) provides an opportunity to aspirants who have completed their Diploma in relevant/ related fields of Design & Technology to secure direct admission in the third semester of UG programmes offered at NIFT campuses.

Q. What is the fees for NIFT?


A. NIFT fee to be paid by non-NRI candidates at different campuses for the first semester and second semester is Rs 1,52,850 and Rs 1,18,050, respectively. Candidates can go through course-wise NIFT fee in the table below:


BDes: Rs 11.09 lakhs

MDes: Rs 5.35 lakhs

Q. How many counselling rounds are there for NIFT admissions 2022?


A. Multiple counselling rounds are conducted for NIFT admissions. In 2021, total two counselling rounds were conducted in online mode through Zoom meetings.

Q. Is there a management quota in NIFT?


A. No, there is no management quota for NIFT admissions. However, candidates belonging to NRI/ OCI/ Foreign Nationals/ SAARC category can apply for admission in design courses offered at NIFT.

Q. When will be the NIFT Entrance Exam 2022 be conducted?
A. The NIFT 2022 exam is slated to be conducted in offline mode on February 6, 2022. However, given the COVID-19 pandemic situation, the exam will now be held as an online, proctored exam. Also, a new exam date may be announced on January 23.

Q. When will the application form of NIFT 2022 open?
A. The application form of NIFT 2022 was open from December 3 to January 17. However, with late fees, applications was accepted till January 22, 2022.

Q. How can I get admission at NIFT?
A. To secure admission in courses offered at the National Institute of Fashion Technology, candidates need to give NIFT entrance exam.

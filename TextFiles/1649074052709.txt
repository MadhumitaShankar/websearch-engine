What is WBJEE?
West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination (WBJEE) is a state-level exam conducted by WBJEEB. The exam is a gateway for candidates seeking admission into and courses at universities, government colleges and self-financing engineering & technical institutions in West Bengal. Last year, a total of 73119 students appeared for the exam out of whom 72298 were ranked. Out of the 72000 students who were ranked, 55154 were male and 17144 were female students.

WBJEE 2022 Eligibility Criteria
WBJEEB has prescribed certain eligibility criteria for WBJEE aspirants. Candidates are required to comply with WBJEE eligibility criteria 2022 to apply for the Joint Entrance Exam successfully. Candidates failing to comply with the eligibility norms will not be allowed to appear for the exam. WBJEE eligibility criteria might vary for different courses and at the institute level.
Take a look at the WBJEE 2022 eligibility criteria below.

Eligibility criteria for appearing in WBJEE 2022

Nationality

The aspirant must be an Indian citizen

Age restriction

A candidate must be at least 17 years of age (as of December 31, 2022) to appear for the exam or be born on or before December 31, 2005.
There is no upper age limit for appearing in the exam.
However, for admission to the Marine Engineering Course, the lower age limit is 17 years as of December 31, 2022, and the upper age limit is 25 years as of December 31, 2022.

WBJEE 2022 Eligibility criteria for B.Tech admissions

Aspirants should have passed Class 12 or equivalent with Physics and Mathematics along with any one of the listed below subjects as compulsory subjects with individual pass marks (in both theory and practical wherever applicable) in all the three subjects in regular class mode.

Chemistry
Biotechnology
Biology
Computer Science
Computer Application
Technical Vocational Subject

Aspirants must have secured a minimum of 45 per cent marks (40 per cent in the case of candidates belonging to reserved categories such as SC, ST, OBC-A, OBC-B, PwD) in the above three subjects taken together.
Candidates should have qualified English in the Class 12 exam with at least 30 per cent marks.
The Board of the Qualifying Examination must be recognised by the Central Government or State Government concerned.

WBJEE 2022 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q. When will WBJEE 2022 be held?
A. WBJEE 2022 will be conducted tentatively on April 30.

Q. Why is it important to verify WBJEE eligibility criteria before applying for the exam?
A. candidates need to comply with the WBJEE eligibility norms. Candidates, who do not meet the minimum eligibility requirements, will not be allowed to appear for the exam and their applications will be cancelled by WBJEEB.

Q. When will WBJEE 2022 application form be released?
A. WBJEE 2022 application form was extended until January 16.

Q. Will WBJEE 2022 be postponed?
A. As of now, WBJEE 2022 has not been postponed by the authorities.

Q. Is WBJEE conducted in online or offline mode?
A. WBJEE is conducted offline in pen and paper-based test mode.

Q. What is the duration of WBJEE 2022?
A. The duration of WBJEE 2022 is 4 hours.

Q. Where can I check the official WBJEE 2022 syllabus?
A. The official WBJEE 2022 syllabus is available online.

Q. What is GMR?
A. GMR is the General Merit List released by WBJEEB for admission to all the Degree Level Engineering / Technology / Architecture Courses available in all enlisted Engineering / Technology Colleges and Universities within the state of West Bengal.

Q. When will WBJEEB declare WBJEE counselling 2022?
A. WBJEEB will announce the WBJEE counselling dates after the result is announced.

Q. Why is WBJEE written exam important?
A. Admissions to the B.Tech and B.Pharma courses offered at colleges in West Bengal are done through the WBJEE exam. This makes it mandatory for candidates to appear for the exam and then participate in the e-counselling for seat allotment.

Q. What are the scholarship schemes offered in West Bengal through WBJEE?
A. WBJEE aspirants are offered scholarships under the Tuition Fee Waiver (TFW) schemes of WBJEEB. The scholarship applies to those students who have performed well in academics and the WBJEE exam. These schemes are applicable for candidates seeking admission to the UG courses in Engineering/Architecture/Pharmacy. The amount of scholarship lies between Rs 1,400 to 1,500 per month.

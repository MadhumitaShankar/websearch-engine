Design Courses in Hyderabad Eligibility Criteria



There are number of design colleges in Hyderabad that offer various design courses. In order to get admission in design colleges in Hyderabad, candidates must possess the below given eligibility criteria at UG and PG levels:



UG Design Courses:
In order to get admissions in a UG level design course, aspirants must have a 10+2 pass
certificate from a recognised board with any of the following streams Science/ Commerce/ Arts.



PG Design Courses:
In order to get admission in a PG level design course, candidates should have a graduation degree from a recognised college.

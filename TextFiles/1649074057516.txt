Fashion Designing is considered as an exciting career option in India. Aspirants can pursue Fashion Design at undergraduate and postgraduate levels. Aspirants can pursue degree, diploma or certificate courses in Fashion Design.
In order to become a successful Fashion Designer, candidates are advised to pursue a bachelor’s degree in design. Some popular courses that candidates can pursue in Design to become a Fashion Designer include Bachelor of Design (BDes) in Fashion Design, Bachelor of Fashion Technology (BFTech), BSc in Fashion Design, Fashion Merchandising, etc.

Top Fashion Design Colleges in India Eligibility Criteria
can be pursued at UG and PG levels. The eligibility criteria differs as per the course and branch selected by the candidate. However, given below is a broad eligibility criteria for UG and PG Fashion Design courses for the reference of candidates:
Fashion Design courses

: Candidates from any stream (Science/ Commerce/ Humanities) are eligible to pursue a Fashion Design course offered at the UG level, provided they have completed their Class 12 from a recognised institute and board. The courses duration ranges from three to four years depending on the course.
UG level
: To pursue a Fashion Design course at PG level, aspirants should have completed their graduation with passing marks from a recognised institute in a related field. The courses duration is of two years.

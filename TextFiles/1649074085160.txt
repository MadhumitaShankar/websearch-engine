Design Courses in Chandigarh Eligibility Criteria



There are number of design colleges in Chandigarh that offer various design courses. In order to get admission in design colleges in Chandigarh, candidates should meet the below mentioned eligibility criteria at UG and PG levels:



UG Design Courses:
In order to get admission in design course at UG level, candidates should have 10+2 pass certificate from a recognised board with any streams like Science/ Commerce/ Arts.



PG Design Courses:
In order to get admission in a PG level design course candidates should have a graduation degree from a recognised college.

RRB NTPC 2022 FAQs

Q: When RRB NTPC CBT 1 2022 revised result will be announced?
A: RRB NTPC CBT 1 2022 revised result has been announced on March 30.

Q: When RRB NTPC 2022 second stage CBT will be conducted?
A: RRB NTPC 2022 second stage CBT will be conducted in May for Level 6. The exam for other levels will be conducted after a reasonable gap.

Q. How can I prepare for the RRB NTPC CBT 2 exam?
A. You can prepare for the RRB NTPC CBT 2 exam by practicing sample papers or previous years’ question papers for every topic. Study regularly, keep formulas and steps to solve problems at your fingertips. Practice and revise properly for the exam. Also, attempt mock tests to improve your speed and accuracy.

Q. What is RRB NTPC CBT 2 syllabus?
A. The RRB NTPC CBT 2 syllabus comprise questions from topics-General Awareness, Mathematics, and General Intelligence and Reasoning. The topics are basically Class 10, Class 12, and Graduation levels, keeping in view the nature of the post.

Q. Are NCERT books enough for RRB NTPC preparation?
A. Questions asked in the RRB NTPC exam are generally of school level. Candidates can brush up on the topics prescribed for science subjects from the NCERT books of Classes 7 to 10.

Q: What are RRB NTPC Level 5, 6 posts?
A: The posts in Level 5 in RRB NTPC include Goods Guard, Senior Commercial cum Ticket Clerk, Senior Clerk cum Typist, Junior Account Assistant cum Typist and Senior Time Keeper. RRB NTPC Level 6 posts include Commercial Apprentice and Station Master.

Q. Which books are best for RRB NTPC for CBT 2?
A. There are many books available in the market for RRB NTPC CBT 2 . However, candidates should choose the books that have covered the entire syllabus and are written in simple language.

Q. Is there any normalisation of marks in RRB NTPC exam?
A. Yes, RRBs follow normalisation procedure to calculate the scores of candidates. This is done to adjust the difficulty level of question papers as the exam is conducted in multiple sessions and days.

Q: Is there minimum qualifying marks in RRB NTPC 2022 CBT 2 exam?
A. Yes, there is a minimum qualifying cut off in RRB NTPC 2022 CBT 2 exam. The minimum qualifying marks for UR & EWS categories is 40 per cent, for OBC and SC is 30 per cent and for ST category is 25 per cent.

Q: Is RRB NTPC and Group D exams are same?
A: RRB NTPC and Group D exams are not the same. RRB NTPC exam is conducted to select candidates for Non-Technical Popular Categories (NTPC) graduate and undergraduate posts. While the RRB Group D exam is conducted to select candidates for various posts at RRC Level 1

Q: Which one is better RRB NTPC or RRB Group D?
A: RRB Group D is the entry-level job in the Indian railway while RRB NTPC comes under the category of group C jobs, which are next to Group D posts. For RRB Group D posts, the educational qualification required is a Class 10 pass. For RRB NTPC undergraduate posts, candidates must have passed Class 12, while for the graduate posts, candidates must have passed Graduation.

Q: What is the full form of RRB NTPC?
A. The full form of RRB NTPC is Railway Recruitment Board Non-Technical Popular Categories.

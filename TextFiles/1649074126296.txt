APSET 2021 result of the certificate verification will be announced soon. The second phase of APSET certificate verification was held on January 5, 2022, for candidates who could not attend or complete the verification in the first phase. The first phase of APSET certificate verification was held from December 20 to 22, 2021. Candidates who qualified the exam need to appear for the verification of documents basis on which the Andhra Univerisity will issue the certificates. The APSET result and scorecard were released on November 22. The University released the roll numbers of the candidates who have been provisionally declared qualified in the exam, subject to the fulfilment of APSET eligibility criteria. The exam was held on October 31, 2021, in eight regional centres of the state.

What is APSET?
Andhra Pradesh State Eligibility Test (APSET) is conducted by on behalf of the Government of Andhra Pradesh to determine the eligibility of candidates for the post of Assistant Professors or Lecturers in the colleges and universities of the state. The exam is conducted for a total of 30 subjects as mentioned below.

APSET 2021 Exam Highlights
Have a look at the APSET 2021 exam highlights below.







Name of exam

Andhra Pradesh State Eligibility Test (APSET)


Conducting body

Andhra University, Vishakhapatnam


Exam level

State


Exam frequency

Once a year


Exam mode

Offline


Exam duration

180 minutes


No. of papers & total marks


Paper 1: 100 marks
Paper 2: 200 marks


Total questions


Paper 1: 50 questions
Paper 2: 100 questions


Marking scheme


+2 for each correct answer
There is no negative marking


Language of exam

English and Telugu


Exam Fees


INR 1200 for General
INR 1000 for BC

APSET 2021 Exam FAQs

Q. When will APSET result 2021 release?
A. The APSET result 2021 was released by Andhra University on November 22, 2021 along with the scorecard and cutoff marks.

Q. What is the use of APSET?
A. The APSET stands for
Andhra Pradesh State Eligibility Test which is conducted
to determine the eligibility of candidates for Assistant Professors or Lecturers in the colleges and universities of the state.

Q. Who conducts the APSET exam?
A. Andhra University, Vishakhapatnam
conducts the APSET exam on behalf of the Government of Andhra Pradesh.

Q. Is APSET a recruitment exam?
A. No, APSET is not a recruitment exam. Candidates who clear the exam become eligible to apply for the recruitment of
Assistant Professors or Lecturers in the state colleges and universities.


Q. Is the APSET exam conducted online or offline?
A. The APSET exam is conducted offline. The exam is held in offline mode and candidates need to mark their answers on the OMR sheet.

Q. What is the APSET examination process?
A. The APSET exam process comprises various stages such as the release of APSET notification, filling of application form, APSET admit card, the conduct of APSET exam and release of the result.

Q. How to apply for APSET 2021?
A. Candidates need to fill the online APSET application form 2021 to apply for the exam. Andhra University released the APSET application form on August 11. Candidates could fill the form till September 13 without paying a late fee.

Q. In how many subjects the APSET exam is conducted?
A. The APSET exam is conducted for a total of 30 subjects.

Q. What is the APSET exam pattern?
A. The APSET exam pattern comprises Paper 1 and Paper 2. In Paper 1, a total of 50 MCQs are asked and in Paper 2 100 MCQs are asked. Paper 1 is common for all candidates and Paper 2 is conducted in 30 subjects.

Q. How do I qualify APSET exam?
A. You need to obtain minimum qualifying marks to qualify the APSET exam. The minimum qualifying marks for the APSET exam are 40 per cent for the General category and 35 per cent for
OBC (NCL)/SC/ST/PwD category.

Q. Is there any negative marking in APSET?
A. No, there is no negative marking in the APSET exam.

Q. When is the APSET result declared?
A. The APSET result is generally declared within two months of the exam. The result is declared online on the official website.

Q. What is the process after qualifying APSET exam?
A. After qualifying the APSET exam, candidates are eligible to apply for the post of Assistant Professor only in the universities/ colleges situated in Andhra Pradesh.

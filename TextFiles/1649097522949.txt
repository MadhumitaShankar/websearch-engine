The is a group of Wikipedians that encourage and assist with the development of WikiProjects. The council acts as a central point for inter-WikiProject discussion and collaboration. New to WikiProjects? See for the answers to the most common inquiries. The outlines generally accepted protocols and conventions.

How can you help?


There's no formal list of participants here, so there's no need to sign up. To help out, just monitor the and join ongoing discussions! You can also help to update the manual by checking to see if section listings are up-to-date.

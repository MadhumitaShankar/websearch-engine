Law is defined as the art of justice. It is the discipline that deals with customs and rules of conduct of a community. It involves mainly two domains Public law and Private law. Public law includes criminal law, constitutional law and administrative law while Private law deals with commercial law.



Law has become one of the most-popular fields among candidates as it provides ample number of career opportunities. Popular law colleges in Orissa accepts score obtained by students in examinations including CLAT, AILET, LSAT India, KIITEE, SAAT, CGET and many more.

Law Colleges in Orissa Eligibility Criteria



There are multiple colleges that offer Law courses in Orissa. But candidates need to know about the eligibility criteria before applying to the law course. Given below is the eligibility criteria for UG and PG levels:




Eligibility for UG Law Courses:


In order to secure admission in UG law courses, candidates must have passed 10+2 or its equivalent exam from a recognised board with minimum 45% marks.




Eligibility for PG Law Courses:


In order to get admission in LLB (3 years) candidates need to have a graduate degree from a recognised university. For LLM course, candidates need to have LLB or equivalent degree.

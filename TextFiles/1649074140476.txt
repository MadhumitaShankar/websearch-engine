What is CEPTAM?
DRDO CEPTAM is conducted by the Centre for Personnel Talent Management (CEPTAM) of Defence Research & Development Organisation (DRDO). CEPTAM is a corporate body of DRDO established as Personnel Assessment Centre (PEACE) to cater to DRTC Cadre (Technical Staff) of DRDO. The centre is responsible for the recruitment of Technical, Administrative & Allied category staff, assessment and facilitates training for this cadre.
CEPTAM conducts the exam for recruitment of Technical, Administrative & Allied category non-gazetted personnel.

Highlights of DRDO CEPTAM
Check below some of the important highlights of DRDO CEPTAM exam.




Exam Particulars

Exam Details


Exam Name

DRDO CEPTAM


Conducting Body

Centre for Personnel Talent Management (CEPTAM) of DRDO


Exam Level

National level


Exam Frequency

Vacancy based


Mode of Exam

Online


Exam Stages

Tier 1, 2 and Document Verification (As per posts)


Exam Fees

INR 100 for General and OBC candidates
SC/ST/Women/PwD/ESM candidates are exempted from paying the application fee


Exam Duration

For Tech A and A&A posts, Tier-I is held for 120 minutes
For MTS post, Tier-I and II are held for 90 minutes


No. of Papers and Total Marks

MTS Post (100 Marks each for Tier I and II)
Tier-I comprises questions from General Intelligence & Reasoning Ability, General Awareness and Quantitative Aptitude & Numerical Ability
Tier-2 comprises questions from General Science, General Math and General English
Tech A (150 Marks)

Part A comprises questions from Quantitative Ability/Aptitude, General Intelligence & Reasoning Ability, General Awareness, English Language (basic knowledge), General science

DRDO CEPTAM 2022 FAQs

Q. Is DRDO CEPTAM 2022 MTS exam cancelled?
A. Yes, DRDO CEPTAM MTS exam has been cancelled. The exam was earlier scheduled to be conducted in 2021, however; it was postponed by the examination authority due to COVID-19 pandemic. The recruitment notification was released in December 2019.

Q. How can I apply for DRDO CEPTAM?
A. Candidates need to apply online for DRDO CEPTAM. The application form is released separately for different posts.

Q. What is DRDO CEPTAM Tech A recruitment?
A. DRDO CEPTAM Tech A recruitment is the exam conducted for the posts such as Carpenter, Draughtsman (Mechanical), Electrician, Fitter, Turner, Welder etc.

Q. What is DRDO CEPTAM STA 'B' recruitment?
A. The recruitment for DRDO CEPTAM STA ‘B’ is conducted for the post of Senior Technical Assistant ‘B’ under Defence Research & Development Organisation Technical Cadre (DRTC).

Q. What is DRDO CEPTAM A&A recruitment?
A. DRDO CEPTAM A&A recruitment is conducted for the post of Stenographer Grade-II (English Typing), Administrative Assistant ‘A’ (English Typing), Administrative Assistant ‘A’ (Hindi Typing), Store Assistant ‘A’ (English Typing) and Store Assistant ‘A’ (Hindi Typing), Security Assistant ‘A’, Clerk (Canteen Manager Grade-III), Asstt Halwai-cum Cook, Vehicle Operator ‘A’, Fire Engine Driver ‘A’ and Fireman.

Q. What is DRDO CEPTAM MTS recruitment?
A. DRDO CEPTAM MTS recruitment is conducted to fill up the vacant posts of Multi Tasking Staff (MTS), General Central Service Group ‘C’, Non-Gazetted Ministerial post.

Q. What is DRDO CEPTAM exam pattern for MTS?
A. The exam pattern for MTS post is divided into two Tiers. Tier 1 is for screening, while tier 2 is for final selection. Both tier 1 and 2 exams are conducted in Computer Based Mode. Tier 1 comprises questions from General Intelligence & Reasoning Ability, General Awareness and Quantitative Aptitude & Numerical Ability. Tier 2 consists of questions from General Science, General Math and General English.

Q. What are the age criteria for applying to the various posts under DRDO CEPTAM?
A. The age limit for DRDO CEPTAM is different for different posts. The age limit for Tech A post is 18 to 28 years. The age limit for MTS post is between 18 and 25 years. The age limit for A&A post is between 18 and 27 years.

Q. Is there negative marks in DRDO CEPTAM?
A. No, there is no negative marking in DRDO CEPTAM. The scores of candidates are calculated based on the number of correct answers.

Q. Is there any qualifying marks in DRDO CEPTAM exam?
A. Yes, there are qualifying marks in DRDO CEPTAM exam. The minimum qualifying marks for tier 1 are 40 per cent for UE/ESM/OBC and 35 per cent for SC/ST candidates.

Q. What is the required educational qualification for applying to DRDO CEPTAM?
A: The educational qualification is different for different posts. For Tech A post, the required educational qualification is Class 10 with certification from ITI in relevant trade. For A&A post, candidates applying to the posts of Clerk, Asstt Halwai-cum Cook, Vehicle Operator, Fire Engine Driver, Fireman must have passed Class 10 while for Stenographer, Administrative Assistant ‘A’ (English Typing), Administrative Assistant ‘A’ (Hindi Typing), Store Assistant ‘A’ (English Typing), Store Assistant ‘A’ (Hindi Typing), Security Assistant posts, the educational qualification is Class 12. For MTS, candidates must have passed Class 10 or equivalent from a recognised board or Industrial Training Institute (ITI).

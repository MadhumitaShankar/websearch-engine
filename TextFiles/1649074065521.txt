Design is one of the most popular courses in India. is a career stream that can be pursued by candidates at undergraduate (UG), postgraduate (PG) or doctorate (PhD) level to become designers. It offers vast streams and several opportunities to the students from different backgrounds based on their skills and talent. The duration of Design programmes varies depending on the course pursued. The duration of UG-level degree Design programmes usually ranges from three to four years. On the other hand, the duration of PG-level degree programmes is usually two years.

Design Courses and Colleges in India: Eligibility Criteria
Many Design colleges in India offer design courses at different levels. The candidates must fulfil the following eligibility criteria for admission to Design colleges in India:

UG Level

Candidates must have completed from a recognised board
Class 12
Candidates from any stream, that is, Science, Commerce and Arts, can apply for the course

PG Level

Candidates should have completed from a recognised college/university
graduation
Most colleges prefer graduates with a Bachelor’s degree in Design stream only. However, candidates from other streams are also eligible to apply for the course based on the required skill-set and aptitude.


Note:

The eligibility criteria might vary depending on the course and college opted by the candidate.

PhD Level

Candidates should have completed their Master’s degree in Design or any equivalent degree with maximum 50 per cent aggregate from a recognised college

Note:
The percentage of marks might vary from one college to another.

FAQs

Q1. How many Design colleges are there in India?
A. Roughly, there are 1,900 Design colleges in India. Out of these, over 1,000 are private, 250 are government and 114 are public/private.

Q2. Which private college is best for Design?
A. Pearl Academy, SID and JD Institute of Fashion Technology are the few top private Design colleges in India.

Q3. Which specialisations are offered by Design colleges in India?
A. There are various specialisations offered by Design colleges in India. Some of the top ones include:

Fashion Design
Textile Design
Apparel Design
Graphic Design
Game Design

Q4. Which are the best Fashion Design colleges in India?
A. Some of the include NIFT Delhi, , , and NIFT Patna.
best Fashion Design colleges in India

Q5. Which are the most affordable Design colleges in India?
A. Some of the most affordable Design colleges in India include NIFT Delhi, NIFT Gandhinagar and NIFT Kolkata.

Q6. Is there any scope in higher studies for a Design graduate?
A. Yes, there is good scope in higher studies for a Design graduate. Students can pursue the following courses in their field of their specialisation after completing graduation in Design:


Master of Design (MDes)

Master of Arts (MA)

Master of Science (MSc)

Master of Fine Arts (MFA)
PG Diploma

Q7. What are the eligibility criteria for a PG Design course in India?
A. For PG Design admission, candidates should complete graduation from a recognised college. It may be noted that many colleges prefer graduates with a Bachelor’s degree in Design stream only.

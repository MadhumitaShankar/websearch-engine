SSC JE result 2022 for paper-II was released on February 25. Staff Selection Commission released the result and the category-wise cutoff for all the engineering streams. Along with the cutoff, the commission also mentioned the number of candidates available in each category for the document verification process. 1,865 candidates qualified for the document verification phase. For Civil Engineering, 1,294 candidates qualified and for Mechanical/Electrical, 571 candidates qualified for the next phase. The dates and admit cards for the document verification will be released later. SSC JE Paper-II was conducted on September 26. A total of 5,711 candidates appeared in the exam.

What is SSC JE?
SSC Junior Engineer (JE) is conducted by SSC for the recruitment of Junior Engineers (JEs) in various branches of including , , and Quantity Surveying & Contracts. The exam is conducted to recruit candidates for Group-B (non-gazetted) posts in level-6 (Rs 35,400-1,12,400) of the pay matrix of the seventh Central Pay Commission.

SSC JE 2022 Exam Highlights



SSC JE is a national level exam organised for the recruitment of candidates for the post of junior engineers in the projects of Central Government and Public Sector Enterprises. Following are the highlights of the SSC JE Exam.





Exam Name

SSC JE (Staff Selection Commission Junior Engineer)



Conducting Body

SSC



Exam Level

National



Vacancies

to be announced



Exam Frequency

Once a year



Exam Mode

Paper-1: Online
Paper-2: Offline



Exam Duration

Paper-I: 120 minutes
Paper-II: 120 minutes



Exam Purpose

Selection of candidates for the post of Junior Engineer in ministries/departments/organisations of the Government of India



Exam Language

English and Hindi



Exam Helpdesk No.

011-69999845



Official Website

http://ssc.nic.in/
SSC JE Result
SSC JE final result 2019 was released on January 27, 2022. The commission released the result online in PDF. Along with the result, the commission also released the cutoff of all the categories.
SSC JE Eligibility Criteria
SSC JE eligibility criteria differ from post to post. However, the basic parameters that candidates need to fulfil in order to meet SSC JE 2021 eligibility criteria are:

: A candidate must be either a citizen of India or a subject of Nepal/Bhutan or a Tibetan Refugee who came to India before January 1, 1962, or an Indian who migrated from Pakistan, Burma, Sri Lanka, East Africa countries
Nationality/Citizenship
: A candidate should complete his/her degree/diploma in Civil/Electrical/Mechanical engineering as of January 1, 2021
Educational qualification
: A candidate should be aged a maximum of 32 years on January 1, 2021

SSC JE 2022 FAQs

Q: When will SSC JE Paper-II released?
A: The SSC JE paper-II result (2020) was released on February 25, 2022. Along with the result, cutoffs were also released.

Q: Is the SSC JE 2021 Result Released?
A: Yes, the SSC JE Result 2021 (Paper-I) was declared on June 30. The SSC also released the SSC JE Cut off 2021 along with the result. The candidates can download here the
SSC JE Result 2021 PDF.

Q: How many stages does SSC JE exam contain?
A: The SSC JE exam is conducted in two stages - paper-I and -II.

Q: When did the SSC JE 2020 paper-I answer key release?
A: SSC JE 2020 answer key for Paper-1 was released on April 6. Along with the answer key, the SSC also released the official question paper. SSC JE Paper-1 2021 was held from March 22-24, 2021.

Q: Is there any age limit to apply for SSC JE exam?
A: Yes, the age limit varies as per the post applied by a candidate.

Q: How many times is SSC JE held in a year?
A: Each paper (I and II) of SSC JE exam is held once a year.

Q: In which mode is the SSC JE exam conducted?
A: The paper-I of SSC JE exam is conducted in online mode as a computer-based test. The Paper-II of SSC JE exam is held in offline mode as a pen-and-paper based test.

Q: What is the application fee for SSC JE exam?
A: The application fee for SSC JE exam is Rs 100. However, the fee is exempted for females of all categories and candidates belonging to SC/ST/PwD/ExS.

Q: What is the minimum educational qualification required to apply for SSC JE exam?
A: Candidates should possess a diploma/degree in Engineering to apply for the exam.

Q: Is there any negative marking in SSC JE exam?
A: Yes, there is a negative marking of 0.25 marks for each incorrect answer in SSC JE paper-I.

Q: Is it mandatory to appear for both the papers of SSC JE exam?
A: Yes, it is mandatory to appear for both paper-I and -II of SSC JE exam.

Q: When will SSC JE notification 2020-21 be released?
A: SSC JE 2020-21 notification has been released on October 1. Earlier, the notification was scheduled to be released on August 4 but was postponed.

AMU Entrance Exam 2022 FAQs
What is AMU Entrance Exam 2022?
AMU entrance exam is conducted by for the selection of candidates for admission in various undergraduate and postgraduate courses. The exam is held in offline mode across selected cities in India. The pattern of the entrance exam varies from course to course. Based on the marks of the candidates in the AMU entrance test, a merit list for admission is drawn. Counselling is conducted based on merit and seats are granted for admission in various courses.

AMU Admission Process 2022
The admission process of Aligarh Muslim University is as follows:






Candidates fill and submit the application form for the AMU entrance exam in online mode at the official website of amucontrollerexams.com. The candidates also need to pay the application fee as per their courses.
Online application –
AMU will issue admit cards to candidates who registered and submitted the application form before the last date successfully. The admit card of AMUEE will mention details about the examination, candidates and the allotted test centre name and location.
Issuing admit card –
Candidates have to appear for the examination at the allotted test centre as per the examination schedule.
Appearing for the test –
The University will declare the results of the AMU entrance exam for each course separately. The results will be available in the form of a merit list of qualified candidates as well as waitlisted candidates.
Announcement of results –
AMU will conduct the counselling process of each course based on merit lists and waitlists. The candidates fulfilling eligibility criteria, qualifying criteria and other requirements will be allotted seats.

AMU Entrance Exam 2022 Pattern
The varies from course to course. However, all the admission tests and the departmental admission tests will be held in offline mode only. Candidates have to take the exams in OMR sheets. The medium of all tests shall be English, except where a question paper is set in a language other than English.
exam pattern of the AMU entrance exam
Negative Marking: Incorrect answers shall result in a negative score of 25 per cent of the marks allotted to the question. For instance, if the correct answer to a question would get the candidate one mark, the incorrect answer would take away 0.25 marks. However, the provision of negative marking will not be applicable in the admission tests of PhD and vocational courses under Community College.

AMU Entrance Exam 2022 FAQs

Q: When will AMU entrance exam 2022 application form release?
A: AMU entrance exam 2022 application form will release in June 2022.

Q: What is the last date to apply for AMU entrance exam 2022?
A: The last date to apply for AMU entrance exam 2022 is July 2022 tentatively.

Q: What is the exam date for AMUEE 2022?
A: AMUEE 2022 will be held in September 2022.

Q: Can i apply for AMUEE 2022 is offline mode?
A: No, candidates can apply for AMUEE 2022 in online mode only.

Q: What is the AMU Departmental Test?
A: The university offers admissions to various courses such as MA (foreign languages), BFA, MFA, MIRM, MHRM, MSc, BVoc, PGD Business Management, MCom, etc. through Departmental Tests i.e. candidates have to apply and appear for the test conducted by the concerned faculty.

Q: What are ‘Non-Test’ courses of AMU?
A: In some of the courses offered at faculties of AMU, there will be no admission test/ departmental test. The admissions for these courses will be based solely on qualifying examinations. The concerned department/faculty will share the qualifying examination and admission criteria with the applicants only.

Q: What is the mode of examination for AMU entrance 2022?
A: The entrance exam will be held in pen-paper based mode i.e. candidates need to fill their responses in an OMR sheet only.

Q: Where will the AMU entrance exam be held?
A: The exam will be held at AMU test centres located in seven cities across India. For the department tests, the exam centre will be in Aligarh only.

Q: What is the exam pattern of AMUEE?
A: The pattern of the entrance exam varies from course to course.

Q: What is the syllabus for AMUEE?
A: The syllabus of the entrance exam varies from course to course.

Q: What is the official website for AMUEE?
A: The official website is amucontrollerexams.com.

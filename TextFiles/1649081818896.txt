What is the beta programme for BBC iPlayer on connected TVs and games consoles?




is an experimental area where you can try out new features in our BBC iPlayer application for connected TVs and games consoles and tell us what you think. iPlayer BETA may behave differently than standard iPlayer because we’re still developing the features in BETA.

Beautiful Interiors Northern Ireland
Series 1
Episode 1 of 6







A luxury holiday home is renovated in Portugal and there's a new sun lounge for a house in County Down.

Show more

Series following Northern Irish interior designers as they revamp a variety of properties.
A trip to Portugal transforms a holiday home into a light-filled luxurious space filled with bespoke pieces. Design concepts include a chandelier made of Murano glass for the hallway, a vanity unit with teardrop-shaped mirrors for the bathroom and a media wall with a eucalyptus wood finish for the living room.
There's also a new sun lounge for a house in County Down with custom-made sofas and a footstool.

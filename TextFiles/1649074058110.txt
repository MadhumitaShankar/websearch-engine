Top Fashion Design Colleges in Delhi NCR Eligibility
Given below is the standard eligibility criteria followed by the majority of fashion design colleges in India:


A Fashion Design programme can be pursued at the graduate level by students who have completed their Class 12 in any stream (Science, Commerce, or Arts).

Most popular Design colleges, on the other hand, only allow candidates to apply if they have completed their schooling from a recognised board.

Aspirants who have finished a Design course at the undergraduate level are recommended for admission to Fashion Designing at the postgraduate level.

Which are the most popular Fashion Design Exams accepted by colleges in Delhi NCR?
The most popular entrance exam for Fashion Design admission in Delhi NCR is NIFT Entrance Exam. Through this exam, a candidate can secure admissions to all the NIFTs in India and other private Fashion Design Colleges accepting the exam scores as well. NIFT is followed by the Pearl Academy Entrance Exam. Other entrance exams are: NID Entrance Exam, CEED, SEED and more.

BTech or Bachelor of technology is an undergraduate engineering degree. One can do BTech in several branches of engineering like Automobile Engineering, Aeronautical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Biotechnology, Computer Science and Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electronics & Communication, Electrical and Electronics Engineering and others. It is normally a four-year programme. The basic eligibility to pursue BTech is one should get a minimum of 50% marks in 12th with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics as the main subjects. The study curriculum for both Bachelor of Engineering (BE) and BTech is the same and anyone who completed BTech successfully will be designated as Engineer and they can join job profiles related to their stream like if one completed BTech in computer science and engineering, the person can work as a Software Engineer. Engineers are in demand, they can either join a company or go for further studies. The course duration of BTech is four years.
Engineering Colleges in Kolkata accept the results of WBJEE, JEE Main, WBJEE JELET, CEE AMPAI and others.

Engineering Colleges in Kolkata Eligibility Criteria
To pursue a BTech from Kolkata or any other part of the country, the following is the basic eligibility criteria:


Minimum 50% marks in 12th exam with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as the main subjects.

Engineering Colleges in Kolkata with NIRF Ranking and Shiksha Rating (Aggregate)
The Ministry of Education releases the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) ranking every year to rank the institutes depending on the parameters - teaching, outreach and inclusivity, learning and resources, peer perception, research and professional practice, and graduation outcome. Shiksha Rating is based on the students ranking the institutes based on parameters like value for money, crowd and campus life, how is the curriculum, infrastructure, faculty, course, and placement.

FAQs

Q: What is BTech Engineering?


A: BTech ( Bachelor in Technology) is an undergraduate level study in Engineering. One can pursue BTech in Engineering after 12th class exams. To do Engineering or BTech one must take a national level or state level engineering entrance examination.

Q: What are the specializations of Engineering?


A: The top specialisations of Engineering are:

Computer Science Engineering,

Aeronautical Engineering,

Biotech Engineering,

Electrical Engineering,

Mechanical Engineering,

Civil Engineering

Electronics & Communication Engineering

Q: What are BTech skills?


A: The skills required to perform well in BTech or engineering include verbal and written communication, teamwork, creativity, multi-disciplinary exposure, familiarity with industry standards, core subject knowledge, leadership skills, analytical & creative thinking, and acquaintance with Computers & IT.

Q: What are the job profiles for BTech graduates?


A: Depending on the stream of Engineering, the BTech graduates can work as a Civil Engineer, Automobile Engineer, Chemical Engineer, Production Engineer, Robotics Engineer, Electronics & Communication Engineer etc.

Q: Which are the more popular branches of engineering in terms of scope?


A: The most popular ones are Aerospace Aviation Engineering, Biotechnology Engineering, Computer Science Engineering, Information Technology Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electronics & Communications Engineering etc.

Q: What is the duration of BTech?


A: BTech duration is four years.

Q: Which are the top recruiters of BTech graduates in India?


A: Some of the top recruiters of BTech graduates in India are TATA Consultancy, Bain & Company, Infosys Technologies, Larson & Toubro, IBM Global Services, InfoEdge India Ltd, Accenture Services, Honeywell, HCL Technologies, Samsung, Goldman Sachs, AT Kearney, Qualcomm, Blackstone, Microsoft, Hindustan Unilever Ltd, Cairn, ITC Limited, Mckinsey Knowledge Centre, JP Morgan, Rubrik and Uber, Intel, Apple, EXL, Google, Flipkart, Morgan Stanley, Myntra, Schlumberger, Amazon, Deutsche Bank, HSBC, Procter & Gamble, ICICI, Millennium, Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd, ISRO, Siemens, Sysmex, and Triveni Turbine Limited.

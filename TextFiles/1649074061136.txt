With the boom in the industry and the new and trendy designs being liked by the people, the Footwear industry is heading towards a new upsurge. It is worth noting that the Footwear industry has a prominent place in the Indian economy in terms of employment generation and export earnings. India accounts for 2.6 per cent of the global leather trade sector and ranks 8th in terms of foreign exchange earnings from the industry. The sector has tremendous potential for growth and employment generation due to strong presence of raw material, skilled manpower, innovative technology and allied industries. There is no doubt that India is today the most favorable sourcing and manufacturing base destination for major countries like Italy, Germany, UK, USA, Hong Kong, France, Spain, Netherlands and Belgium. This is the reason why students are also moving towards specialized courses in Footwear Design. Through this course, students are taught to analyze , trends, manufacturing process, material knowledge and technical skills. Eligibility Critera for Footwear Design

The course in Footwear Design can be pursued by students who have completed their class 10+2 level education from recognized schools affiliated to national/state education boards. There is no restriction of stream and students from all streams i.e. // can pursue an undergraduate course in Footwear Design.

Required Skillset for Footwear Design

Footwear Design as a stream requires aspirants to have a creative flair along with an eye for detail. Important skills that candidates should possess for a successful career in the field include:









Aesthetic interest



Good sketching skills




Skill in using Computer Aided Design



Precision




Eye for detail



Ingenuity and practical bent of mind




Knowledge of colors, blending, color terms and codes



Knowledge of textures




Knowledge of foot anatomy and international size chart for Foot



Keen knowledge of worldwide footwear fashion trends

FAQs on Footwear Design

Q. What is Footwear Design?
A. Footwear Design course provides education of designing a footwear from the raw stage to the finished stage by using different processes. The courses with specialisations impart methodologies of manufacturing. From choosing the right material for footwear to giving it an eye-catching design, it includes everything.

Q. What are some courses in Footwear Designing?
A. There are various courses offered in Footwear Designing including, short-term courses, diploma courses, undergraduate courses and postgraduate courses. Candidates can pursue courses like BDes in Leather Design, Diploma in Footwear Design etc.

Q. What is the eligibility for Footwear Design courses?
A. Candidates seeking admissions to UG and Diploma courses should have passed at least class 12th from a recognised institute. For PG diploma courses in Footwear Design, candidates should hold a bachelor degree in any stream from a recognised college or university.

Q, What are the skills required for Footwear Designing?
A. One should have good cognitive skills, creativity skills, quick learning skills, technical skills to make a successful career in this field.

Q. What is the course curriculum for Footwear Designing?
A. The course curriculum is vast and vary according to the the degree being offered. However, some of the common subjects in Footwear Designing are - Design Foundation, Material understanding, Lab Testing, Project Management, Pattern Making & Construction technique, Color concepts of fashion etc.

Q. What are the job opportunities available after completing the Footwear Design course?
A. Those who complete the degree are hired as - Product Managers, Designers etc by various fashion and footwear companies.

Q. What are some good colleges for Footwear Designing courses?
A. Candidates can pursue Footwear Designing courses from: National Institute of Fashion Technology, Government Polytechnic, Footwear Design and Development Institute, Mewar University, Parul University etc.

Q. Are there master level course in Footwear Designing?
A. Yes, candidates can pursue MDes in Footwear Design and Production after completing their graduation in any stream.

Q. Which are the top recruiters for Footwear Designing course?
A. After completing a course in Footwear Design one can work for companies like Adidas, Action, Liberty Group, Bata, Skechers, Lee Cooper etc.

Q. How can I secure admission in Footwear design course?
A. Many colleges conduct an entrance exam in order to shortlist aspirants for admission in courses offered by them. Students can secure admission in a Footwear Design course if they fulfill the eligibility criteria for the course in which they want to secure admission.

Q. How much is the course fee for Footwear Designing?
A. The course fee for various Footwear Designing colleges ranges from INR 50,000 to INR 8 LPA.

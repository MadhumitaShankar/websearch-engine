Asia


We are growing our business in Asia out of our regional office in Singapore, inspiring audiences with intelligent, distinctive content from the BBC through our channel offerings, finished programmes, formats, local productions and consumer products.

Explore region

Australia and New Zealand


The BBC has been part of Australasian life since the 1960s. Based in Sydney, BBC Worldwide ANZ exclusively owns six channels, distributes great content to every kind of platform, works with local producers to re-develop cherished BBC formats and partners new digital platform operators.

Explore region

Europe, Middle East & Africa


We provide unforgettable content for local markets in EMEA, from South Africa to Slovenia. This includes gripping dramas, intelligent but irreverent shows like Top Gear and the wonders of the universe in our natural history content. Teams in EMEA also build partnerships with local companies to create new content.

Explore region

Latin America


Latin America is one of the world's fastest-growing TV markets. We're tapping in to its huge potential by offering more channels to customers, producing imaginative digital content, strengthening local production relationships, and selling programmes, consumer products and live events. All this from our regional headquarters in Miami.

Explore region

North America


BBC Worldwide North America takes the best of the BBC to the United States and Canada. It is home to our editorial teams for BBC.com and BBC Worldwide Productions, and is also responsible for building powerful new partnerships in the region through our content sales and co-productions teams. Our offices across the United States and Canada bring the unforgettable moments of BBC content to life for adult and children's audiences.

Explore region

United Kingdom


The BBC was born in the UK - and the UK is at the heart of BBC Worldwide. Its diverse activities to grow the BBC brand and drive business opportunities include providing digital downloads, branded products and turning much-loved brands into live events.

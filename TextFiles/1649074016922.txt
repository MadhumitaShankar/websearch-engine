UPCET 2022 will likely be conducted in the fourth week of June 2022. The UPCET 2022 application forms will likely be released in the first week of April 2022. The application window will likely be open for approximately two months and will close in the last week of May 2022.

Note: UPCET 2022 will not be valid for BTech and BArch admissions as it will be done on the basis of JEE Main and NATA scores respectively. MBA course admissions in institutes affiliated with AKTU will be done on the basis of CMAT scores.

What is UPCET 2022?
Known earlier as UPSEE, Uttar Pradesh Combined Entrance Test (UPCET) is a gateway for candidates seeking admission to various professional courses offered at the undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) level at all affiliated institutes of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Lucknow, Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology (MMMUT), Gorakhpur and Harcourt Butler Technical University (HBTU), Kanpur. The exam will be conducted by National Testing Agency (NTA) in the computer-based test mode across various cities.

UPCET 2022 Eligibility Criteria
General UPCET 2022/UPSEE Eligibility Criteria

There is no age limit.
Candidates should have passed the qualifying exam from any institution in UP or the parents should have the domicile of Uttar Pradesh.
If the candidates have not passed the qualifying exam from an institution in UP, then they will be required to produce their parent's domicile certificate.
Candidates who do meet the domicile requirements will also be eligible to appear for the exam if they meet the other required criteria.
Candidates in the final year of the qualifying exam or are awaiting results are also eligible to appear for UPCET.
UPCET 2022 Eligibility Criteria for MCA (Integrated)

Candidates should have qualified intermediate examination of U.P. Board or Class 12 level examination or its equivalent with Physics and Mathematics/Statistics/Accountancy as compulsory subjects and one of the optional subjects - Chemistry/Biotechnology/Biology/Technical Vocational subjects.
Should have obtained at least 45 percent aggregate (40 percent aggregate in case of candidates belonging to reserved category) in the above subjects taken together.
UPCET 2022 Eligibility Criteria for BPharm

Should have passed (with/without grace) intermediate examination of U.P. Board or Class 12 level examination or its equivalent with Physics and Chemistry as compulsory subjects along with Mathematics/Biology subject.

UPCET 2022 Eligibility Criteria for BHMCT/BFAD /BFA/B Voc/ BDes/MBA (Integrated)

Candidates must have passed (with/without grace) the intermediate examination of the U.P. Board or the Class 12 level examination or its equivalent.
Should have secured a minimum of 45 per cent aggregate (40 per cent aggregate for SC/ST category candidates).

UPCET 2022 Eligibility Criteria for MCA in any institution affiliated to AKTU, Lucknow

Candidates should have passed BCA/ Bachelor degree in Computer Science Engineering or equivalent Degree or passed BSc. /BCom/ BA with Mathematics at Class 12 level or at graduation level.
Should have scored a minimum 50 per cent aggregate (45 per cent for SC/ST).

UPCET 2022 Eligibility Criteria for BTech Lateral Entry

Candidates who have passed (with/without grace) three years Engineering Diploma from an Institution located in UP with minimum 45 percent aggregate (40 percent for SC/ST) OR whose parents (Father or mother) are domicile of Uttar Pradesh and he/she has passed three years Engineering Diploma with minimum 45 percent aggregate (40 percent for SC/ST) recognised by the Board of Technical Education, U.P., in any branch of Engineering (except Agriculture Engg., Pharmacy and Architecture).
Candidates who have passed (with/without grace) three years B.Sc. degree and passed Class 12 with mathematics as one of the subjects from any recognised University; securing minimum 45 per cent aggregate (40 per cent for SC/ST) in aggregate. These candidates shall be considered only after filling the supernumerary seats in this category with the candidates belonging to the Diploma stream.
Admission to the second year of B.Tech (Lateral entry) of HBTU would be governed by the eligibility criteria laid down by HBTU.

UPCET 2022 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: Will the UPCET 2022 application forms be available offline?
A. No, the UPCET 2022 application forms will only be available online. Candidates can fill out the application forms online on the official website.

Q: Who conducts the UPCET 2022 exam?
A: The UPCET exam will be conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA), for admission to various undergraduate and postgraduate programmes.

Q: What is the basic eligibility criteria for appearing for UPCET 2022?
A: Candidates who wish to appear for UPCET 2022 must have secured at least 45% aggregate in Class 12 in the required subjects.

Q. Is there any age limit for UPCET 2022?
A. There is no age limit for admission to the undergraduate and postgraduate courses covered in UPCET 2022.

Q: Can I get admission to BTech courses through UPCET?
A: No, according to the latest updates admissions to BTech courses will not be provided through UPCET to colleges in Uttar Pradesh. Candidates need to appear for JEE Main to be eligible for admissions to BTech courses.

Q. Is it important to have an Aadhaar card to fill UPCET application form? What is the application fee?
A: Yes, candidates need to enter their Aadhar number in the online application form of UPCET. The application fee for UPCET 2022 exam is INR 1,300 for the General category and INR 650 for the reserved categories.

Q: Is there any fee concession for reserved category candidates?
A: Yes, candidates belonging to reserved categories and women candidates will be given a fee concession. The application fee for them is INR 650.

Q: Can I make corrections in the UPCET application form after submission?
A: No changes can be made once the UPCET application form has been submitted. Candidates must fill the application form carefully and check the details before submitting the application form.

Q: How many test cities can I choose while filling the UPCET 2022 application form?
A: Candidates can choose up to three test centres while filling the UPCET 2022 application form.

Q: Does the UPCET 2022 exam have negative marking?
A: No, UPCET 2022 exam does not have negative marking. Each question carries four marks.

Q: What is the cutoff percentage set by UPCET?
A. Last year, the cutoff percentage of marks in UPCET for the merit of successful candidates was 25% (20% for SC/ST) as per the UP State Government.

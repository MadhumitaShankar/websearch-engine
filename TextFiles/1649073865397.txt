FAQs

Q: Which are the best colleges in Bangalore for MBA?
A: The best MBA college in India – IIM Bangalore (IIMB) is located in the city. Apart from IIMB, the city is home to SIBM, Presidency College, ICFAI IBS, Christ University, NMIMS, Reva University, Jain University, GIBS Business School, PES University, among others.

Q: Do MBA colleges provide campus placements?
A: Yes, all the MBA colleges in India provide campus placements to a full-time MBA batch. The placement process is conducted in the final year, after the second last semester of the course.

Q: Which are the various specialisations offered by MBA Colleges in Bangalore?
A: Top 10 specialisations offered by MBA colleges in Bangalore are:

Finance
Human Resources
Sales and Marketing
International Business
Healthcare & Hospital
IT and Systems
Operations
Entrepreneurship
Business Analytics
Retail

Q: Which is the best college for MBA in Bangalore based on placements?
A: The top MBA colleges in Bangalore based on placement review rating on Shiksha are:

Regional College of Management (4.9)
IIM Bangalore (4.8)
AIMS IBS (4.7)
ISB&M Bangalore (4.6)
Dayananda College of Arts, Science & Commerce (4.5)
RV Institute of Management (4.4)
Padmashree Group of Institutions (4.4)
SJES College of Management Studies (4.4)

Q: Which are the job profiles offered after completing MBA?
A: Some of the job profiles that are offered by companies after completing MBA are:

Finance Manager
Marketing Manager
Sales Manager
Human Resources Manager
Operations Manager
Product Manager
Data Analytics Manager
Digital Marketing Manager
Advertising Sales Manager

Q: What is the estimated fee of MBA course in Bangalore?
A: It is estimated that pursuing MBA in Bangalore is a bit expensive as approximately 32% of the colleges have course/annual fees of more than Rs 5 lakh. Some of these colleges are:

Indian Institute of Management Bangalore
Indian Institute of Plantation Management
CMS Business School
ISBR Business School
Regional College of Management Bangalore
Institute of Health Management Research
Jain University

Q: What are the popular exams for admission to MBA colleges in Bangalore?
A: Popular entrance exams accepted by Bangalore colleges for MBA admission include KMAT, MAT, CAT, Karnataka PGCET, CMAT, XAT, ATMA, NMAT, etc.

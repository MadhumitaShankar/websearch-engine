Where can I find system information for my connected TV or games console?




You'll need open the BBC iPlayer app on your Tv or console, then head to the Settings menu.
1. Go to
Settings & Help


2. Then go to
Help


3. Click on
System Information


4. On the System Information screen, take note of the following:

: The version of BBC iPlayer that is running - e.g. 4.3.11
BigScreen Version
The device that you're using.
Device:
: The example shows Live. You should always be on the Live Environment.
Environment


If you need to contact us about a potential fault this information will be useful and will help us investigate your issue.

6 Music Festival 2022











Little Simz

Newly crowned BRIT Award winner and Mercury Prize nominee Little Simz gave us everything we wanted and more! Watch her full set now on BBC iPlayer.









Johnny Marr

Manchester's iconic guitar hero plays a career-spanning closing set at the Great Hall









Unmissable moments from 6 Music Festival 2022

Best bits from the triumphant return of this year's 6 Music Festival in Cardiff.








Wet Leg

Tunes so catchy they may never leave your head, it's the indie duo on everyone’s lips








Ibibio Sound Machine

Ibibio Sound Machine’s live shows are something you need to see for yourself. An absolute force of West African funk and disco.








Lucy Dacus

One of the best lyricists in rock today, Lucy Dacus had Cardiff’s Great Hall in the palm of her hands.








beabadoobee

Her first performance since her debut album Beatopia dropped, beabadoobee delivered a real treat at The Great Hall.

Watch on iPlayer, Listen on Sounds









IDLES

Bristol’s famous sons whip the Great Hall into a frenzy. There’s nothing like an IDLES gig.








Mykki Blanco

A genre-defying, trailblazing artist, Mykki Blanco took over St David’s Hall the way only she can.








Pixies

Inspirations to countless bands, icons of punk rock Pixies performed live for 6 Music Festival.








Ezra Collective

A jazz quintet with soulful influences, the superbly talented Ezra Collective set the tone in St David’s Hall Saturday night.








Bloc Party

An incendiary Great Hall set from the edgy art-rockers








Watch more on iPlayer

More highlights from 6 Music Festival 2022 including Lucy Dacus, Deyah and Curtis Harding.









Hear more on BBC Sounds

Listen to 6 Music Festival late sets and live performances from the festival so far.

More from 6 Music Festival









Sports Team

Sports Team brought all the energy to their set in The Great Hall. Mercury nominated and oozing confidence, this isn’t one to be missed.








Deyah

We are blessed to have Cardiff rapper Deyah performing as part of 6 Music festival this year!








BBC Music Introducing at 6 Music Festival

Highlights from Horizons/Gorwelion & BBC Introducing Presents at Clwb Ifor Bach with Adwaith, Panic Shack, Malan & Hemes.









Gruff Rhys Artist Collection

From the archives, a selection of Gruff Rhys interviews with 6 Music through the years, alongside a selection of his favourite tracks









Ibibio Sound Machine Artist Collection

A selection of Ibibio's sessions and interviews at 6 Music, going from their first live session in 2014, right through to a 2022 chat with Rad Mac ahead of 6 Music Festival





6 Music Festival Fringe

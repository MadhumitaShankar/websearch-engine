Law Colleges in West Bengal Eligibility Criteria


There are various law colleges in West Bengal that offer Law courses. Before applying to the courses, aspirants need to check if they meet the eligibility criteria. Given below are the eligibility criteria for UG and PG level courses:



Eligibility for UG Law Courses:
To get admission in UG law courses, candidates must have passed class 12
th
or its equivalent exam from a recognised board with at least 45% marks.



Eligibility for PG Law Courses:
To get admission in LLB (3 years) courses, candidates need to have a graduation degree from a recognised university. For admission to LLM courses, candidates must hold a LLB or equivalent degree from a recognised university.

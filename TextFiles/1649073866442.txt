Master of Business Administration (MBA) is one of the most popular post-graduate programmes. It is an internationally recognised degree designed for individuals who want to pursue a career in business and management. There are nearly 150 MBA Colleges in Chennai. Chennai, being the centre of IT industry and informational services, provides great job opportunities. Along with this, there are several multinational companies in Chennai which offer competitive packages as compared to other cities. Thus, Chennai is one of the prominent regions for students who wish to pursue an MBA. Around 91% of MBA colleges in Chennai are private and the rest are public/government.

MBA Colleges in Chennai Eligibility Criteria
The eligibility criteria to pursue a full-time MBA are as follows:

The candidate must have completed graduation in any discipline or equivalent from a recognised university.
The candidate must have a minimum aggregate percentage of 50% in graduation. For reserved category students, the minimum aggregate percentage is 45%.
Final-year graduation candidates can also apply for MBA provided they present the proof of completion of the Graduation degree within the duration as specified by the institute.

Note:
The minimum aggregate percentage might vary from college to college.

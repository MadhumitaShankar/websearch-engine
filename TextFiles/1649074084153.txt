Design Courses in Gujarat Eligibility Criteria



There are numerous design colleges in Gujarat that offer various design courses. In order to get admission in design colleges in Gujarat, candidates should meet the eligibility criteria required at UG and PG levels:




UG Design Courses:
To get admissions in a UG level design course, students should have passed 10+2 from a recognised board with any streams such as Science/ Commerce/ Arts.




PG Design Courses:
To get admission in a PG level design course, students should have a graduation degree from a recognised college.

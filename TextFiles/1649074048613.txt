Admission Process


NEET
The Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) is one of the most sought courses after Class 12 in the medical field. It is an undergraduate degree program in the medical field that makes the person a certified medical practitioner. The duration of the program is 5.5 years including one year of rotational internship at hospitals, healthcare centers, and health camps.
During one year of MBBS internship, students can get training in hospitals and healthcare centers as consultants, physicians, medical assistants in critical care units, etc. On completion of the MBBS internship, students can register as doctors with the Medical Council of India (MCI).
MBBS course includes the study of human anatomy, medicine, human cytology, pharmaceutical, chemistry, drugs formulation, and surgical effect and method.
Pune is home to a total of eight medical colleges that offer admission to MBBS on the basis of scores obtained in the NEET examination.
Medical Colleges in Pune Eligibility Criteria
The eligibility criteria for admission to top medical colleges in Pune differ from college to college. Have a look at the basic eligibility criteria below.

Candidates must qualify the NEET exam
Candidates must have passed Class 12 (Science) exams with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/ Zoology/ Botany subjects.
Candidates must have studied English as the main subject in Class 12 for their qualifying examination
Candidates also need to fulfill the age limit criteria, that is, they must have attained 17 years of age at the time of admission
There are additional eligibility conditions as mentioned by the regulatory authorities such as the Medical Council of India (MCI)

A.

Scientific research and development Skills
Perseverance towards professional commitments and medical ethics
Medical writing skills

Q. What is the scope of study after doing MBBS from Medical colleges in Pune?
Students can get training in hospitals and healthcare centers or work in the health sector as qualified doctors after pursuing MBBS from the medical colleges of Pune. They can also appear for Combined Medical Services Examination for employment in central government organizations. Moreover, Medical students can also pursue higher studies abroad.
A.

Q. Is the NEET score required for the MBBS course in Pune?
A. Yes, the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) score is required for admission to the MBBS course in medical colleges of Pune.

Q. Can I do MBBS from Medical colleges in Pune after Class 12?
Yes, you can take admission to the MBBS course after completing 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English. You must also secure a minimum aggregate of 50% in the core subjects (PCB).
A.

Q. What is the duration of the MBBS degree course in Pune?
The total duration of an MBBS degree course from Medical Colleges in Pune is 5.5 years. To complete the MBBS degree course, students need to complete two stages: 4.5 years of academics and 1 year of internship.
A.

Q. Can one use "Dr." after completing MBBS from Medical Colleges in Pune?
Yes, once you clear the final examination or qualifying examination of MBBS from Medical Colleges in Pune along with the rotatory internship of 1 year, you can officially add "Dr" to your name.
A.

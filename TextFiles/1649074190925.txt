Law is a career stream that candidates pursue at undergraduate (UG), postgraduate (PG) or doctorate (PhD) level to practice legal profession in India. Law as a profession offers a plethora of career avenues for students to explore and conquer. The preferred specialisations in Law are either Criminal or Civil. However, these days other branches are also gaining popularity such as Cyber Law, Patent Law as well as Corporate Law. Students looking forward to a career in Law can opt from UG, PG, integrated or PhD degrees. Students looking forward to a career in Law have a wide range of options at both UG, PG, PhD and dual/ integrated degrees.

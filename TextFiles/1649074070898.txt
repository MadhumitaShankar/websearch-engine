What is NID Entrance Exam?
NID Design Aptitude Test (DAT) is a national-level entrance exam through which aspirants are shortlisted for admission to UG and PG Design courses offered by the prestigious . Candidates need to clear two NID admission tests in order to be eligible for admission to Bachelor of Design (BDes) and Master of Design (MDes) courses offered at its campuses.
National Institute of Design (NID) campuses
Initially, NID offered , Graduate Diploma Programme in Design (GDPD), and courses at its campuses. However, from 2020 onwards, the few NID campuses (NID Andhra Pradesh, NID Madhya Pradesh NID Haryana, and NID Assam), which offered GDPD courses also started offering BDes degree programs to students based on their performance in the NID Entrance Exam.

NID Admission Process 2022




NID DAT 2022 Selection Process
NID admissions 2022 is a two-tier process. To secure admission to BDes and MDes courses offered at NID campuses, candidates need to appear for NID DAT (Prelims) and NID DAT (Mains). The objective of conducting both the tests is to assess knowledge, skills as well as behavioural abilities of candidates. Aspirants can go through the steps included as part of NID admission process 2022 below:

Step 1: Fill NID DAT Application Form
Candidates need to go through the eligibility criteria for NID admissions to the course in which they want to apply. After doing this, aspirants need to fill their on the official website of National Institute of Design. Along with the application form, candidates need to upload their supporting documents, signature and photograph. After filling all the details in NID registration form, aspirants need to pay the application fee either online (by Credit/ Debit Card) or offline (by Demand Draft to be drawn in favour of "All India Management Association" and should be payable at New Delhi).
NID DAT 2022 application form

Step 2: Download
NID DAT Admit Card
The call letter/ is made available for download on the official website of the exam. Aspirants who do not receive the admit card for the entrance test need to contact NID Admission Cell immediately.
admit card for NID Entrance Exam

Step 3: Appear for NID DAT (Prelims)
The first stage of NID admissions, NID DAT (Prelims) is a written test, which comprises objective as well as subjective questions. The entrance exam conducted by NID is of three hours duration. Check complete .
NID DAT exam pattern

Step 4: Check NID DAT Prelims Result
Candidates can check their on the official website of the exam by providing details such as their email address, date of birth, roll number/application number.
result for NID entrance exam

Step 5: Download NID DAT Mains Admit Card
Candidates who clear NID DAT Prelims exam are then issued an admit card for NID DAT Mains. Aspirants can download NID DAT Mains admit card from the official website of National Institute of Design by entering their login details.

Step 6: Appear for NID DAT Mains
Candidates who make it in the cut-off list for NID DAT (Prelims) are then eligible to appear for NID DAT (Mains), wherein authorities conduct a studio test and interview round. In the studio test for NID admissions, aspirants are given hands-on practical assignments, which they need to complete within the stipulated time.

Step 7: Check NID Final Result
Based on a candidate's performance in NID DAT (Prelims) and NID DAT (Mains), the final merit list for NID admissions is prepared. Aspirants can check the merit list for NID admissions on the official website of the exam.

FAQs on NID Entrance Exam

Q. When will NID DAT 2022 Prelims results be declared?
A. NID DAT 2022 results for the prelims exam are expected to be declared anytime now.

Q. When will NID DAT 2022 be held?
A. NID DAT 2022 exam dates have been released. The NID DAT Prelims was held on January 2.

Q. How can I secure admission at NID?
A. You need to appear for NID Entrance Exam, which comprises two admission rounds - NID DAT Prelims and NID DAT Mains. You will be allowed to appear for NID Mains only if you clear the Prelims exam.

Q. What is NID DAT Prelims?
A. NID DAT Prelims is the shortlisting round for NID admissions. NID DAT Prelims comprises a written exam that candidates need to clear in order to be eligible for NID DAT Mains.

Q. What is NID DAT Mains?
A. NID DAT Mains is the final round for NID admissions. NID DAT Mains consists of a Studio Test and Interview round that candidates need to clear in order to secure admission to Design courses offered by NID campuses.

Q. What is the cutoff for NID admissions?
A. In 2021, the Open category candidates had to secure minimum 50 marks in order to be eligible for NID admissions.

Q. How can I prepare for NID DAT?
A. Candidates need to have a thorough prep strategy in place when appearing for NID DAT. NID Entrance Exam comprises objective as well as subjective questions. Candidates who clear NID DAT Prelims then need to appear for NID DAT Mains which is the material handling round for NID admissions. Aspirants can go through tips and tricks to formulate .
NID Entrance Exam Prep strategy here

Q. How many students appear for NID DAT?
A. The statistics for the number of students who took NID Entrance Exam 2021 have not been released. However, in 2019, a total of 21,032 students applied for NID DAT. Data shows that more females apply for NID Entrance Exam as compared to male candidates. In 2019, 13,891 female candidates applied for the design entrance exam as compared to 7,933 male aspirants.

Q. How many seats are there for NID admissions 2022?
A. Total seats have increased for NID admissions. Seats available for admission to BDes courses through NID Entrance Exam is 425. Apart from this number, overseas candidates are offered 19 seats for admission to BDes course. For MDes course, around 347 seats are available for admission at NID campuses. Apart from this number, some seats are also reserved for overseas (supernumerary) candidates.

Q. Which NID campus is best?
A. National Institute of Design (NID) has seven campuses. Aspirants can secure admission to BDes courses at NID Ahmedabad, NID Andhra Pradesh, NID Haryana, NID Madhya Pradesh and NID Assam. MDes courses are available for admission at NID Ahmedabad, NID Gandhinagar and NID Bengaluru.

Q. What kind of questions are asked in NID Entrance Exam?
A. NID Entrance Eexam consists of two admissions tests – NID DAT Prelims and NID DAT Mains. NID DAT Prelims is a written entrance exam and NID DAT Mains comprises a studio test and interview round. In NID DAT Prelims, candidates are asked both objective- and subjective-type questions.

Q. Is NID a government institute?
A: National Institute of Design (NID) has been declared as an 'Institute of National Importance' by a bill passed in the Lok Sabha. Established in 1961, NID is an autonomous national institution.

Q. What is the fees of NID?
A. NID fee to be paid by candidates for BDes course (without hostel facilities) is Rs 1,51,050. MDes course fee to be paid by Indian nationals is Rs 1,75,580.

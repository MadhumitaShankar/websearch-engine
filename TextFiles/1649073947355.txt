Industrial engineering is an engineering profession which deals with the development, improvement and implementation of integrated systems of knowledge, information, equipment, human resources, and finance to optimize the complex engineering processes or systems and organizations, dealing in engineering work. Industrial engineering professionals works to improve the overall efficacy of the engineering systems by analyzing, evaluating and implementing the best suitable methods.
Industrial engineering is closely associated with production engineering which intends to work towards improving the overall industrial production by design, development, implementation, operation, maintenance, and control of all processes in the manufacture of a product. Industrial and production engineering course are mostly studied as a single specialization with institutes offering production and industrial engineering degree.

FAQs Regarding Industrial Engineering

Industrial Engineering as a career
Industrial engineering professionals work in every engineering industry, be it mechanical, electrical, electronics or computers. There are enormous job opportunities for industrial engineering professionals as they are required in almost every organization engaged in production or manufacturing. The industrial engineering skill are very much required in increasing the overall efficiency of production by minimizing the operating cost, planned use of manpower and other resources. The future prospect of industrial engineering is also very bright considering the increase in demand from production units which would lead to rise in such professionals.

E
ligibility for Pursuing Industrial Engineering Courses
Candidates interested in pursuing industrial engineering course need to fulfill certain eligibility criteria. The eligibility criteria varies for the level of courses and will be different for industrial engineering degree, industrial engineering certifications and industrial engineering diploma.

Eligibility for Industrial Engineering Degree Course


Candidates seeking admission in graduate courses need to have passed class 12 with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics as compulsory subjects

Candidates interested in masters course need to have passed engineering graduate course with relevant branch

The requirement of percentage of marks in the course varies from institute to institute.

Candidates should qualify the entrance exam of the institution through which the institute offer admission.

Candidates interested in pursuing industrial engineering course from IITs need to qualify JEE Advanced while those interested from pursuing industrial engineering degree from NITs, or CFTIs need to qualify JEE Main.

Eligibility for Industrial Engineering Diploma Courses


Candidates should have passed full-time Engineering/Technology degree in relevant branch from a recognized university with aggregate 60%

Candidates should have qualified GATE exam with a valid score.

Eligibility Criteria for Industrial Engineering Post Graduate Degree Courses


Candidates should posses full time graduate degree (B.Tech) in the relevant field of engineering

The requirement of percentage of marks in degree course varies from institute to institute.

Candidates should have a valid score in GATE exam

Institutes offering Industrial Engineering Courses
There are many institutes across the country offering industrial engineering courses at various levels. Candidates can select the best institute as per the level of courses they want to pursue.

Industrial Engineering Skills
The industrial engineering professionals posses such skills which would help in increasing the efficiency of industrial production and at the same time reduce the operating cost. Following are some of the industrial engineering skills


Coordinate and implement quality control objectives, activities or procedures to resolve production problems, maximize product reliability, or minimize costs

Estimate production costs and the effects of product design changes on expenditures

Communicate with management and stakeholders to develop production and design standards

Review production schedules, specifications, orders, and related information to learn about manufacturing methods, procedures, and activities

Recommend cost-saving measures

Use drafting tools and computer-assisted design software to create the layout of equipment, materials, and workspace to illustrate maximum efficiency

FAQs Regarding Industrial Engineering

Is Industrial Engineering a good career?
Industrial engineering has enormous career opportunities as industrial engineers are required in every organisation engaged in production or manufacturing. Industrial engineering skills are very much used in industries to maximise the production and profit and reduce operating cost.

What kind of job do industrial engineering professionals do?
Industrial engineers make
work to maximize the quality of a product or service by
developing integrated systems of materials, machines, energy, information, and people
. They also create financial management programs that
budgeting and cost analysis less time-consuming

Is industrial engineering in demand?
industrial engineers
As the industries will continue to seek new ways to contain costs and improve efficiency, the employment opportunities for industrial engineering is very high.
Employment of
is projected to grow 10 percent from 2019 to 2029, much faster than the average for all occupations.

What skills do industrial engineers need?
Industrial engineering skills are critical thinking and creative, problem solving and learning new methods, team player beside having the knowkedge of core subjects.

Where can I study industrial engineering?
There are many courses which offer industrial engineering courses like IIT Delhi, IIT Bombay, National Institute of Industrial Engineering among others.

Which industrial engineering course is best to get a job?
Candidates can opt for B.Tech in industrial engineering or can go for diploma courses offered by many institutes.

Which companies hire industrial engineering professionals?
Companies which are engaged in production or manufacturing of goods be it electrical, mechanical, electronics or computer or common household items, industrial engineering professional are required there.

What is the salary of industrial engineer?
The salary of industrial engineer can vary depending up on the course done, professional skill and experience. At entry level, the salary range can vary from 3 lakh per annum to 5 lakh per annum.

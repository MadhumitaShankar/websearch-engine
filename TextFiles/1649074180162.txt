Entrance-based
Law is a popular course choice among Indian applicants interested in becoming lawyers. Candidates can enrol in law school after graduating Class 12 and getting a bachelor's degree. Enrol in one of the five-year integrated-LLB programmes, such as BA LLB, BBA LLB, BSc LLB, or BTech LLB, if you want to continue your studies beyond Class 12. Those interested in going into law after graduation can enrol in three-year LLB programmes. The LLM is a legal post-graduate degree.

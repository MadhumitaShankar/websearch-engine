What is RPF SI exam?
RPF SI exam is conducted by the Ministry of Railways to select eligible male and female candidates for recruitment to the post of Sub-Inspectors (SI) in Level 6 of 7th CPC in Railway Protection Force (RPF) and Railway Protection Special Forces (RPSF) in various zones of Indian Railways.

RPF SI 2021 Exam Highlights




Exam name

RPF SI


Conducted by

Ministry of Railways


Post name

Sub-Inspectors (SI) in Level 6 of 7th CPC


Level of exam

National


Groups

Group A, B, C, D, E and F


Exam category

Graduation


Stages of exam


Computer Based Test (CBT)
Physical Efficiency Test (PET) & Physical Measurement Test (PMT)
Document Verification


Mode of exam

Online


Exam duration

90 minutes


Language of test

Hindi, English, Urdu, Tamil, Telugu, Konkani, Malayalam, Kannada, Marathi, Gujarati, Bengali, Odia, Assamese, Manipuri and Punjabi


Exam purpose

Recruitment of SI in RPF and RPSF


Official website

https://indianrailways.gov.in/







RPF SI Eligibility Criteria 2021
The eligibility criteria of RPF SI provide details regarding age, educational qualification and nationality. Candidates can check below RPF SI eligibility:

A candidate must be a citizen of India
Any graduate candidate can apply for the exam
The age of candidates should be between 20 to 25 years

RPF SI 2021 FAQs

Q. How will I apply for RPF SI exam?
A. RPF SI application form is released on the official website. Candidates need to apply online for the exam.

Q. What is the exam fee for RPF SI exam?
A. The exam fee is INR 500, out of which an amount of INR 400 is refunded after deducting bank charges on appearing in the CBT. For candidates belonging to SC/ST/Ex-Serviceman/ Female/ Minorities / Economic Backward Class, the exam fee is Rs 250 which is also refunded after deducting the bank charges.

Q. What is the process after selection in RPF SI exam?
A. Selected candidates will have to undergo rigorous initial training in any one of the RPF/RPSF training centres or any other such institution as decided by the railway administration. The trainee recruits/cadets are given a stipend of INR 35,400 plus other allowances.

Q. How would I know my RPF SI registration is complete?
A. There are five stages in RPF SI registration. On completion of all these stages, you will get an email and SMS alert.

Q. What is the standard of RPF SI syllabus?
A. The standard of the RPF SI syllabus is of graduation level.

Also Read:
RPF SI syllabus

Q. How many RPF SI application forms can I submit?
A. Candidates need to submit only one application form for RPF SI exam.

Q. When RPF SI exam dates will be announced?
A. RPF SI notification is yet to be announced. The exam dates will be released along with the notification.

Q. In how many languages RPF SI exam is conducted?
A. RPF SI exam is conducted in 15 languages: Hindi, English, Urdu, Tamil, Telugu, Konkani, Malayalam, Kannada, Marathi, Gujarati, Bengali, Odia, Assamese, Manipuri and Punjabi

Q. What is the marking scheme in RPF SI exam?
A. For the correct answer, candidates score one mark. For incorrect answers, 1/3 marks are deducted as penalty.

Q. What is the salary of candidates selected through RPF SI exam?
A. The salary of candidates selected through RPF SI exam is INR 35,400.

Q. What are the qualifying marks for RPF SI exam?
A. To qualify RPF SI CBT, candidates belonging to General and OBC categories need to obtain 35 per cent marks while it is 30 per cent for SC and ST candidates.

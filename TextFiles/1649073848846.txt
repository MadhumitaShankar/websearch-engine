Pune, known as ‘Oxford of the East’ as it is an academic centre of national repute and is one of the most sought-after MBA study destinations. There are more than 220 best MBA colleges in Pune that offer quality education and good placements. The city has a lot to offer to MBA aspirants with a plethora of job and entrepreneurial opportunities. Pune is also home to some of the top-ranked MBA colleges.
MBA course in Pune is offered in full-time, part-time, distance learning and online mode of education. The top MBA colleges in Pune provide degree, diploma and certificate courses and are ranked by organisations such as NIRF, Outlook and Times.
Top MBA Colleges in Pune Eligibility Criteria
The eligibility criteria for an MBA course in Pune may differ from college to college. However, the basic eligibility criteria to pursue an MBA is as follows:

Candidates must have passed graduation in any discipline or equivalent from a recognised university (full-time)
Most of the institutes follow the minimum score criteria in graduation which is 50% aggregate or equivalent. Some of the colleges also offer a 5% relaxation to students from the reserved categories.
Final-year graduation candidates are also eligible to apply for MBA, provided they present the proof of completion of graduation degree within the duration specified by the institute

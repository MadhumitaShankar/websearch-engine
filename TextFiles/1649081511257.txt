How can I contact BBC iPlayer Support or give feedback?




We try to keep our help site up to date with the latest issues and guidance. However, there may be times when you can’t find an answer to your question or you’ll need to contact us if you’re experiencing an issue that’s not listed. Here's how to get in touch:


Live chat
With live chat you can have a conversation with a BBC iPlayer support agent in real-time to help solve any problems or answer your questions quickly. Check our page to find out how to use live chat.

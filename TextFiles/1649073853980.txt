Important FAQs Regarding CMAT 2022

Q: How many times is CMAT exam held in a year?
A: The CMAT is held once a year in the month of January. The exam is conducted at various exam centres across 124 cities in India. You can choose the test city according to your preference. The CMAT 2022 exam date is April 9.

Q: Is CMAT conducted online or offline? Will it be delayed due to COVID-19?
A: CMAT exam is conducted in computer based test mode. Candidates are required to punch in their answers with the help of keypad and mouse of the computer system provided. Yes, CMAT 2022 has been delayed. It will be held on April 9.

Q: When is the last date to apply for CMAT 2022?
A: The CMAT 2022 registration started from February 16 and the last date to apply is March 17. Candidates can make fee payment till March 18, up to 11:50 PM. Candidates must fill the forms within the deadline as no extension of last date is expected.

Q: I have scored less than 50 per cent marks in Graduation. Am I eligible to apply for CMAT?
A: Yes, you are eligible to apply for CMAT. Any candidate who has secured passing marks in graduation can apply for CMAT. Candidates must ensure that they have cleared graduation or first two years of bachelor's degree to be able to apply for CMAT.

Q: For how long is CMAT score valid for admission to MBA institutes?
A: Validity of CMAT score is one year. For example, CMAT 2022 score is valid for admission to 2022-23 academic session only. If you take the exam in a given academic year but do not take admission, then you will have to reappear for CMAT in the next academic session.

Q. What does it mean by CMAT participation institutes?
A: The CMAT participating institutes are the institutions which accept the CMAT score for admission to MBA/PGDM courses. These colleges shortlist candidates for admission on the basis of CMAT score.

Q: Is there any age restriction for appearing in CMAT?
A: There is no upper age restriction for appearing in CMAT exam. However, since the eligibility criteria for CMAT is graduation, the candidate's minimum age should be 20.

Q: What is the reservation criteria for CMAT?
A: There is no reservation criteria for CMAT. However, CMAT participating institutes have to abide by reservation criteria for educational institutions set by the Government of India.

Q: What is the CMAT 2022 application fee for SC/ST/PwD students?
A: CMAT 2022 application fees for SC/ST students is as follows:

Boys: INR 2,000
Girls/reserved categories: INR 1,000

Q: Is there a provision of rechecking or revaluating CMAT answer sheets?
A: No, there is no provision of rechecking or revaluating CMAT answer sheets. Marks allotted to candidates in the scorecard will be final. If any candidate wishes to increase their CMAT score, they will have to appear for the exam again in the next session.

Q: What is NTA-CMAT Test Practice Centre?
A: The NTA has established a network of Test Practice Centres spread across India to help the students practice and train themselves for CMAT exam in online mode. This is to help the students acclimatise themselves with the online test process.

Q: Till when will CMAT 2022 result be available for download on the official website?
A: The CMAT result will be preserved up to 60 days from the date of declaration on the CMAT-NTA website. Candidates must download their scorecards before that. After two months, they will have to put special requisition with the exam authorities for CMAT scorecard.

Q: Will I receive CMAT 2022 result and scorecard through post?
A: CMAT 2022 result will be available online only on the CMAT official website. No hard copy of the scorecard will be sent to any candidate. Thus, the candidates must ensure they download the result PDF from the CMAT website as soon as the result is announced.

Q: Will the NTA release CMAT 2022 merit list after result declaration?
A: Yes, the NTA is expected to release CMAT 2022 merit list along with the result. The merit list will be released in the form of PDF, containing each test taker's section-wise scores, overall score and overall percentile.

Q: Is there any difference between CMAT score and percentile?
A: Yes, CMAT score is the total marks one has scored in the exam, while CMAT percentile is their rank compared to all other test takers. To understand these two concepts well,
read this article:
CMAT Score vs Percentile: How to Calculate?

Q: How many times can I attempt CMAT?
A: Since CMAT is an annual exam, you can attempt it only once in the particular year. However, you can take the exam in the consequent academic sessions. Score of the last CMAT exam will be taken into account at the time of admission.

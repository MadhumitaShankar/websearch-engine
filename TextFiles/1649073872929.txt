FAQs on MBA Colleges in Pune

Q: Which are the best MBA college in Pune for in Marketing?
A: Some of the best MBA colleges in Pune include MIT WPU, MIT College of Management, UNIPUNE-Savitribai Phule Pune University and ASM’s Institute of Business Management & Research among others.

Q: In which MBA colleges of Pune, MBA Finance is offered?
A: MBA colleges in Pune offering Finance as specialisation include International Institute of Management Studies, MIT WPU, Lexicon Management Institute of Leadership & Excellence, IndSearch Institute of Management & Research, ISMR and AIMS Institute of Management Studies among others.

A: Popular MBA entrance exams accepted for admission to MBA colleges in Pune include CAT, CMAT, MAT, XAT, ATMA, MAH CET, SNAP, NMAT, etc.

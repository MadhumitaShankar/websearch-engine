Dental or dentistry is a specialisation or branch of medical sciences, that deals with oral health. To put it simply, Dental courses can be referred to as the study of teeth, gums and other organs in the mouth. Generally, Dental courses are taught at medical colleges at degree level, such as Bachelors or Masters in Dental Surgeries. A number of students or aspirants plan to pursue Dental Diploma or Dental Certification courses.


Dental is a vast branch of Medicine that teaches students about the various oral organs, their structures, dental anatomy, diseases and their diagnosis and treatment. Dental syllabus, course curriculum and specialisations vary from course to course and institute to institute. However, the core curriculum or Dental subjects are the same.

The eligibility criteria for the Dental courses vary from programme to programme. Let us take a look at the programme-wise eligibility criteria of Dental courses.

Candidates should pass Class 12 in Science stream with Physics, Chemistry and Biology as core subjects



They should obtain minimum 50 per cent aggregate (General category students) and 45 per cent (reserved category candidates)



Their age should range between 17 years and 25 years

Admission Process for Dental Courses


Admission to Dental courses is conducted on the basis of entrance exams. The admission process can be divided into the following steps:




Eligible aspirants have to apply for the Dental entrance exams

Q: What is the duration of Dental courses?



A: The duration of the Dental courses vary from programme to programme and also according to the levels. BDS, which is a full-time UG level course is of a four-year duration. MDS or the Master of Dental Surgery, which is a postgraduate programme, is of two to three years duration. The Dental Diploma level programmes are for two years, whereas the duration of Dental Certificate programmes is six months to one year.

Q: What are the top courses in Dental?



A: The top or the most preferred Dental courses are the full-time BDS and MDS programmes.

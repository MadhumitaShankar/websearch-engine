What is ACLAT 2022?
ACLAT is an online entrance test for admission to the LLM course offered by . The admission test. ACLAT is not hed for admission in BA LLB (Hons) and BBA LLB (Hons) courses. Admission to these UG courses is based on AUSAT. Alliance School of Law, Alliance University also uses other parameters such as scholastic and extracurricular records for admission. The final selection of the candidate will be based on the overall performance in the Admission Selection Process.

ACLAT 2022 Eligibility Criteria
Check the eligibility for law admissions in Alliance School of law below:

For BA LLB (Hons)

Passed/appearing in 10+2 or equivalent examination from a recognized Board.

Minimum of 45% marks in aggregate (40% marks for SC/ST candidates).

Age Limit: For General Category - 20 years and for SC / ST and Backward Communities - 22 years.

For BBA LLB (Hons)

Passed/appearing in 10+2 or equivalent examination from a recognized Board.

Minimum of 45% marks in aggregate (40% marks for SC/ST candidates).

Age Limit: For General Category - 20 years and for SC / ST and Backward Communities - 22 years.

For LLM

Passed/appearing in the final year of LLB/BL or any other graduate degree in Law recognized by Bar Council of India.
Minimum of 45% marks in aggregate (40% in case of candidate belonging to reserved category).
ACLAT 2022 Admission Process
The candidates who qualify in ACLAT and AUSAT have to appear for the PI round. Apart from the entrance test and PI. Alliance School of Law, Alliance University also uses other parameters such as scholastic and extracurricular records for admission. The final selection of the candidate will be based on the overall performance in the Admission Selection Process.

ACLAT 2022 FAQs

Q: Who conducts ACLAT?
A: Alliance University conducts ACLAT for admission in the LLM course.

Q: Is the ACLAT 2022 application process open?
A: Yes, ACLAT 2022 application form is available.

Q: Is there any entrance test for BA LLB (Hons) and BBA LLB (Hons) admission?
A: Yes, Alliance University holds the
AUSAT (Alliance University Scholastic Aptitude Test) for BA LLB (Hons) and BBA LLB (Hons) admission.

Q: What is the eligibility for ACLAT 2022?
A: ACLAT 2022 eligibility is
LLB/BL or any other graduate degree in Law recognized by Bar Council of India with a minimum of 45% marks in aggregate (40% in case of candidate belonging to reserved category).

Q: What is the application fee for ACLAT 2022?
A: ACLAT 2022 application fee is
INR 1000/- (US$50 for other foreign national applicants).

Q: In what mode is ACLAT conducted?
A: ACLAT 2022 will be held in online mode.

Q: What is the admission process for BA LLB (Hons), BBA LLB (Hons) and LLM courses at Alliance University?
A:
The candidates who qualify in ACLAT and AUSAT have to appear for the PI round. Apart from the entrance test and PI. Alliance School of Law, Alliance University also uses other parameters such as scholastic and extracurricular records for admission. The final selection of the candidate will be based on the overall performance in the Admission Selection Process.

Q: What is the fee of Alliance School of Law?
A: The BA LLB (Hons) and BBA LLB (Hons) fees are INR 10,00,000 for 5 years. The LLM fee is INR 2,00,000 for 1 year.

Q: What is the eligibility for BA LLB (Hons) and BBA LLB (Hons)?
A: The eligibility is
10+2 or equivalent examination from a recognized Board with a minimum of 45% marks in aggregate (40% marks for SC/ST candidates) for BA LLB (Hons) and BBA LLB (Hons).

Q: Is there any age limit for admission in BA LLB (Hons) and BBA LLB (Hons)?
A: Yes, there is.
For General Category - 20 years and for SC / ST and Backward Communities - 22 years.

Q: What is the official website for ACLAT 2022?
A: The official website is alliance.edu.in.

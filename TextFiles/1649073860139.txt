What is KIITEE Management?
KIITEE Management is a national-level computer-based entrance examination conducted by for admission to various MBA courses. The test is held across 15 cities in India. Candidates have to answer 150 questions in 150 minutes. Based on the scores obtained in the examination, candidates will be called for Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interviews (PI) rounds. Candidates having , , , , and scores were not required to appear for KIITEE MBA exam.

KIITEE MBA Eligibility Criteria

The candidate must be a graduate in any discipline with 50 per cent aggregate marks from any university or possess an equivalent qualification recognized by the Ministry of HRD, Government of India.
The candidate must have 50 per cent aggregate marks (or equivalent CGPA) in the Class 10 and 12 examinations. Those who are awarded CGPA or grades in place of marks, their CGPA will be converted into equivalent marks based on the equivalence certified by the university/institution/board from where they obtained their degree/certificate. In absence of any instruction from the university, equivalence would be established by dividing obtained CGPA with the maximum CGPA and multiplying the fraction so obtained by 100.
Candidates appearing for the final examination for the Bachelor’s degree (or equivalent examination) are eligible to apply. However, they will have to clear their final exam of Bachelor’s degree by June 30, 2022.
Marksheet and other documents of passing the final year of Bachelor's degree is to be submitted by September 2022. Failing to submit the requisite documents in time will entitle a student to withdraw from the programme.
Foreign/NRI candidates with above mentioned eligibility criteria are required to submit the filled-up application form along with the requisite documents and application fee of US $50. They will be considered for admission process on the basis of their valid GMAT scores.
Domestic candidates who possess valid scores of CAT, XAT, ATMA, CMAT, MAT will be directly eligible for Group Discussion and Personal Interview.




KIITEE Management 2022 Admission Process
KIITEE MBA 2022 admission process will consist of four stages – application, entrance test, result and GD/PI.

STEP 1: KIITEE MBA 2022 Application process
Aspirants can apply online as well as offline. For offline application, candidates can obtain registration form and prospectus after paying the application fees of INR 1,250 in cash/demand draft drawn in favour of “KIIT School of Rural Management” payable at Bhubaneswar.

STEP 2: KIITEE MBA 2022Admit Card

The admit card for KIITEE Management 2022 exam will be made available for download for the registered candidates, which can be downloaded by using login credentials. Admit card is a mandatory document to be carried to the exam hall, hence the candidates must download and take a print out of the same.

STEP 3: KIITEE MBA 2022
KIITEE Management test will be held at 15 test centres across the country. The candidates are expected to reach their respective exam venue one hour before the commencement of exam. The question paper will contain MCQs from Quant, GK, English, Analytical Reasoning and Issue of Social Concern

STEP 4: KIITEE MBA 2022 Result
KITTEE MBA result has been released on the official website. Candidates mus can download their scorecards from the official website by using their index number and date of birth. The scorecard contains candidates scores and other details required in GD/PI.

STEP 5: KIIT GD/PI
Candidates successful in KIITEE Management exam will be shortlisted for GD/PI round. Candidates who shall have applied through CAT/XAT/CMAT/MAT/GMAT scores will also be shortlisted for this round. They selected candidates will have to be present in person during GD/PI. Final admission will be granted on the basis of the candidate’s overall performance in the test, GD/PI and academics.Candidates can apply for KIITEE Management exam, in two modes, when the dates are declared.

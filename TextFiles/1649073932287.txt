There are around 120 Engineering colleges in West Bengal, which offer quality education with good placements. These colleges offer degrees through full-time, part-time, or distance modes of learning. According to NIRF ranking 2020, the top Engineering colleges in West Bengal are Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (IIT Kharagpur), Jadavpur University, Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology (IIEST, Shibpur), National Institute of Technology Durgapur (NIT Durgapur), Heritage Institute of Technology, Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology, Haldia Institute of Technology and Institute of Engineering & Management. The best Engineering colleges in West Bengal offer specialisation in Computer Science Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Marine Engineering.
BTech
To pursue a four-year BTech programme from the top Engineering colleges in West Bengal, candidates must have passed Class 12 from a recognised board with Physics, Chemistry, and Maths. Besides, candidates need to clear the national-level or state-level entrance exams such as JEE Main, WBJEE, CEE AMPAI, JEE Advanced, DASA UG, etc.

Top Engineering Colleges in West Bengal Eligibility Criteria
The eligibility criteria for admission to the top Engineering colleges in West Bengal is given below:

Candidates must have passed Class 12 with a minimum 60% aggregate (the qualifying percentage is different for different colleges) from a recognised board with Physics, Chemistry and Maths as core subjects
Best Engineering colleges in West Bengal also accept the scores of national-level and state-level entrance exams such as JEE Main, WBJEE, CEE AMPAI, IEMJEE, JEE Advanced, etc.,

Top Engineering Colleges in West Bengal with NIRF Ranking and Shiksha Rating (Aggregate)
According to NIRF ranking 2020, there are total of 8 top Engineering colleges in West Bengal. Agencies like NIRF rank colleges based on various parameters such as teaching, learning and resources, research and professional practices, graduation outcomes, outreach and inclusivity and perception. Shiksha aggregate rating is given by the students based on five different parameters such as placements, infrastructure, faculty & course, curriculum, crowd & campus life and value for money.

ROI of Top Engineering Colleges in West Bengal
Return on Investment or ROI plays an important factor in choosing a particular course from a particular college. ROI is the comparison between the fee invested by students while a particular course and the return he gets in the form of placement. This factor is very much valued while selecting a college or university. ROI also helps students when it becomes difficult for them to choose two shortlisted colleges.

FAQs

Q1. What are the different specialisations offered by the top Engineering colleges in West Bengal?
A. The top Engineering colleges in West Bengal offer specialisations in Computer Science Engineering, Civil Engineering and Marine Engineering.

Q2. Which are the top Engineering colleges in West Bengal?
A. The top Engineering colleges in West Bengal are Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (IIT Kharagpur), Jadavpur University, Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology (IIEST, Shibpur), National Institute of Technology Durgapur (NIT Durgapur), Heritage Institute of Technology, Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology, Haldia Institute of Technology and Institute of Engineering & Management.

Q3. What are the job opportunities after I complete BTech course from the top Engineering colleges in West Bengal?
A. The job opportunities available for BTech graduates after completing the course from the top Engineering colleges in West Bengal are Engineering Support Specialist, Database Manager, R&D Applications Engineer, Quality Engineer, Web Developer, Software Engineer, Software Quality Engineer, Mechanical Engineer, etc.

Q4. What are the popular BTech entrance exams accepted by the top Engineering colleges in West Bengal?
A. The popular BTech entrance exams accepted by the top Engineering colleges in West Bengal are JEE Main, WBJEE, CEE AMPAI, IEMJEE, JEE Advanced score, etc.

Q5. What is the scope after completing BTech from the top Engineering colleges in West Bengal?
A. After completing BTech from the top Engineering colleges in West Bengal, aspirants can opt for higher studies and pursue courses like MTech/ME, MS, MBA, etc.

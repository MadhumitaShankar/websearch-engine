Karnataka has been recognized as the most rapid economic as well as a technologically developed state. The state also includes Bengaluru which is known as a hub for Information Technology. Apart from Bengaluru, many other districts offer a good platform of education including Manipal, Mysore, Mangalore, etc. BTech Colleges in Karnataka offer quality education with high placements. These colleges offer full-time BTech courses in Karnataka that are your gateway to a career in Engineering. These courses are for a duration of four years. Of the many BTech specialisations, Computer Science Engineering is the most sought-after and offers bright prospects for student growth.

BTech Colleges in Karnataka: Eligibility Criteria
The eligibility criteria for the BTech courses may differ from college to college. However, the basic eligibility criteria for BTech are as follows. The candidates:

Must have passed Class 12 with a minimum 60% aggregate from a recognized board with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects (the pass percentage may vary in different colleges)
Admission is based on merit, written exam, Group Discussion, or Personal Interview and it varies from college to college

Top Engineering Colleges in Karnataka with NIRF Ranking and Shiksha Rating (Aggregate)
National Institute of Ranking Framework (NIRF) gives ranking to colleges based on various parameters like teaching, learning, and resources, research and professional practices, graduation outcomes, outreach and inclusivity, and perception. Shiksha aggregate rating is an authenticated rating given by the students based on five different parameters such as placements, infrastructure, faculty & course, curriculum, crowd & campus life, and value for money. ROI of Best Engineering Colleges in Karnataka
Return on Investment or ROI shows how beneficial it is to pursue a particular course from a particular college. It is the comparison between the fee invested by the student while studying and the return he/she gets in the form of placement. In the case of popular Engineering courses, selecting a college or university based on the ROI factor can be very much valued. This is helpful when it becomes difficult to choose between two shortlisted colleges. National Institute of Technology Karnataka, Surathkal offered a maximum average salary in comparison to the lowest annual fee making it the top Engineering college in Karnataka ROI-wise.

What is the difference between a BE and BTech programme?

The major difference between BE and BTech programmes is with respect to the course material taught and the orientation of the programme. The following table captures the points of difference between these two programmes.
A.





BE


BTech



BE is more theoretical and knowledge-oriented. It is a study based on theory.
The course deals with engineering aspects of Science
The course is applied to develop innovative equipment and useful technical gadgets in Science and Technology


BTech is skill/data-oriented. The course is more of application engineering
The course deals with the technical aspects of Science
The course applies the principles of engineering and technology to modify structures and thereby enhance their quality

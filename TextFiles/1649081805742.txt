How do I change the video quality on my computer or laptop?




BBC iPlayer on computers and laptops automatically defaults to the highest quality picture your internet speed will allow.
This might cause buffering for some people, so to try to fix this, change the picture quality:

Click on the in the playback bar (next to the subtitles icon)

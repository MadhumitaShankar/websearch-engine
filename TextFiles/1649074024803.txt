BITSAT 2022 dates have been released by the Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS), Pilani. Candidates can now fill out and submit the BITSAT 2022 application form for Session I until May 21 (5 PM). The authorities will conduct BITSAT 2022 in two sessions - June 20 to 26 (Session I) and July 22 to 26 (Session II). Only those candidates who have applied for Session I will be eligible to attempt the second session. Registered candidates will be required to book their slots for the exam as only after BITSAT slot booking, candidates will be allowed to download their admit cards. What is BITSAT?
Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test (BITSAT) 2022 is an entrance exam conducted by the Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS), Pilani, for admissions to undergraduate engineering courses (BE) at its three campuses located at Pilani, Goa and Hyderabad. Admissions will be offered to the candidates into BE, BPharm and MSc programmes after qualifying for the admission test. The exam is conducted online and around 3 lakh students apply for this entrance exam every year.

FAQs regarding BITSAT 2022

Q. When will the process of BITSAT 2022 application form filling begin?
A. BITSAT 2022 application form is now available for Session I. For Session II, the application form will be available from June 23.

Q. When will the BITSAT 2022 be conducted?
A. BITSAT 2022 will be conducted in two sessions - June 20 to 26 (Session I) and July 22 to 26 (Session II).

Q. Are class 12 board toppers allowed to participate directly in the BITSAT counselling?
A. First rank holders of all national and state boards are eligible for direct admission to BE courses offered by the BITS Pilani. They need not appear for the entrance exam.

Q. Is the BITSAT score mandatory for B.Pharma admission at BITS campuses?
A. If you are aspiring for BPharma admission in any of the campuses of BITS, you will have to clear the BITSAT exam.

Q. Does any private college (expect BITS) accept a BITSAT score for B.Tech admission?
A. BITSAT score is valid only for BE admission offered by BITS Goa, Pilani and Hyderabad campuses.

Q. Is BITSAT tougher than JEE Main?
A. According to previous trends and experts' feedback, the difficulty level of JEE Main and BITSAT is almost similar.

Q. What will be the medium of the BITSAT 2022 question paper?
A. BITSAT 2022 Question paper will be conducted online in English medium only.

Q. I have taken Physics, Chemistry, and Biology in 10+2. Am I Eligible to Apply for BITSAT?
A. Candidates with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology in 10+2 can attempt BITSAT 2022. However, they will be eligible to apply only to the BPharm programme.

Q. I passed the class 12 exam in 2020 with less than 75% aggregate in PCM. Am I eligible if I reappeared in the class 12 exam to improve my marks?
A. Yes, candidates appearing for the class 12 exams are eligible to apply for the BITSAT.

Q. What was the previous year's cutoff of BITSAT?
A. BITSAT cutoff varies according to the branches offered by participating institutes. Last year, the minimum marks required by candidates for admission to BITS Pilani was 270.

Q. What is the age criteria for BITSAT 2022?
A. As per the age criteria for BITSAT 2022, candidates should have completed their 12th standard exams in 2021 or 2022.

Why do I need to sign in each time on my Samsung TV?







BBC iPlayer |
Update


*** March 2022 ***
We're aware that some users are being asked to sign in each time they use the BBC iPlayer TV app.
Samsung TV
We believe that this is happening because data storage isn't enabled on the TV.
If affected, please on your TV by going to your .
enable data storage
TV Settings > Broadcasting > Expert Settings > HbbTV Settings

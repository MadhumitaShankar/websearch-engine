What to do if you've lost your phone, tablet or laptop
Page updated: 18 July 2017



If anyone tries to open your BBC account settings, we'll always check it’s you by asking for your password first. So even if your phone, tablet or laptop was signed in to your account, your private data will stay safe.
But if you’re still worried, there are two things you can do:

. If you think someone knows your password you can reset it on the "" page.

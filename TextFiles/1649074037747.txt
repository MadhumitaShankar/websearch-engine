What is AIIMS PG 2020 Exam?
AIIMS PG is conducted by AIIMS (All India Institute for Medical Sciences) for admission to MD, MS, DM, M.Ch, MDS. The exam is organised twice a year for filling seats in PG courses in AIIMS, located at New Delhi, Bhopal, Bhubaneswar, Jodhpur, Patna, Raipur and Rishikesh. AIIMS PG entrance examination is conducted in an online mode and follows a negative marking system.

Exam Day Guidelines for AIIMS PG 2020

It is mandatory for all test-takers to carry a valid photo identity proof with them on the day of the examination
Do not carry any electronics such as calculators, mobile phones, smartwatch, etc. on the day of the examination
AIIMS PG exam centres will not have provisions to keep bags or other valuables
Eatables/ beverages/ water bottles are not allowed inside the examination hall
Reach the examination hall at least an hour prior to the examination
AIIMS PG 2020: Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
Q. How many times is AIIMS PG entrance test conducted?
A. The examination is conducted twice a year in an online mode. AIIMS PG is conducted in the months of December and May for January and July sessions respectively.
Q. How many seats are available through AIIMS PG 2020 examination?
A. According to the latest seat matrix released by the Institute, the total number of seats available through the AIIMS PG 2020 January session examination was 548.
Q. What is the application fee for AIIMS PG 2020?
A. The application fee for AIIMS PG 2020 for candidates belonging to general and OBC category is Rs 1500. For those belonging to the reserved category, the application fee for AIIMS PG 2020 is Rs 1200. Candidates who belong to the PWD category are exempted from paying the application fee.
Q. When will the registration for July 2020 session start?
A. The basic registrations for AIIMS PG 2020 July session started on November 27.

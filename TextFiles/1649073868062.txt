MBA Colleges in Delhi/NCR with NIRF Ranking and Shiksha Rating (Aggregate)
National Institutional Ranking Framework, commonly known as NIRF, has been established by MoE to rank institutes across India imparting courses under different streams. NIRF has set a few parameters to consider under the methodology to rank MBA institutes across India. Every year a survey to gauge these parameters and rank the institutes. Parameters under NIRF include

Teaching, Learning and Resources
Research and Professional Practices
Graduation Outcomes
Outreach and Inclusivity
Perception
Alternatively, Shiksha (Aggregate) rating considers five to six parameters to rate colleges out of five. This rating is based on the reviews given by active students and alumni.

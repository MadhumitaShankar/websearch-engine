In Maharashtra, the medical degree is offered across various specialisations including pharmaceutical, , general Medicine, quality assurance, and , pediatrics, and obstetrics & gynecology. For admission into various medical courses, candidates should have passed class 12th standard with science subjects including biology. As healthcare is one of the largest service sectors, it offers huge career scope to students. Maharashtra has more than 100 medical colleges offering various full-time, distance-learning, part-time and online mode of study. Out of these, the top 13 medical colleges in Maharashtra have been ranked by NIRF and India Today that offers degree courses in a full-time mode. Apart from merit-based admission, many top-ranked Medical colleges in Maharashtra also accept scores of national- and state-level entrance exams, including NEET, etc., for Medical admissions.
Top Medical Colleges in Maharashtra Eligibility Criteria
The eligiblity criteria may differ from college to college. However, the basic eligibility criteria for Medicine and Health Science courses have been given below:

Candidates must have passed Class 12 examination with Science subjects i.e. Physics, Chemistry and Zoology / Botany
Candidates must have studied English as the main subject in the qualifying examination
Moreover, candidates also need to fulfill the age limit criteria i.e. they must have completed 17 years of age at the time of admissions
There are additional eligibility conditions such as mentioned by the regulatory authorities for respective discipline i.e. Medical Council of India (MCI), Dental Council of Indian, and Indian Nursing Council, Pharmacy Council of India, Indian Association of Physiotherapist and respective association/council for other medical and health science courses.
Top Medical Colleges in Maharashtra with NIRF Ranking and Shiksha Rating (Aggregate)
Agencies like NIRF give ranking to colleges based on various parameters like teaching, learning and resources, research and professional practices, graduation outcomes, outreach and inclusivity and perception. Shiksha aggregate rating is an authenticated rating given by the students based on five different parameters such as placements, infrastructure, faculty & course, curriculum, crowd & campus life and value for money.

ROI of Top Medical Colleges in Maharashtra
Return on Investment or ROI is the comparative account of investment on course fees and returning against it in terms of placements. This is very crucial and candidates do need to weigh both the aspects (fees and placements) before taking a final plunge. ROI provides candidates an idea of whether they should take up a particular course in a particular college or not. This is helpful when it becomes difficult to choose between two shortlisted colleges.

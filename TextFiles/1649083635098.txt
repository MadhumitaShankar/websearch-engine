A traditional home in Moira gets a radical remodel, including an open-plan living space, and there's a challenging new build design for a property in Helen’s Bay.

Show more

A traditional home in Moira gets a radical remodel, including an open plan living space with an eclectic coffee nook, a leather feature wall with concealed doors, and a new family entertainment room. There is also a challenging new build design of a six-bedroom property in Helen’s Bay. Design features include a new cinema room and an innovative headboard with a control panel to open curtains to a sea view from the comfort of the master bedroom.

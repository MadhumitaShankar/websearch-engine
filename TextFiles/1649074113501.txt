IBPS PO 2022 exam dates have been announced. The mains exam will be conducted on November 26.
The prelims exam of IBPS PO 2022 will be conducted on October 15, 16 and 22.
What is IBPS PO?
Institute of Banking Personnel Selection Probationary Officers or IBPS PO exam is conducted every year for recruitment to the post of Probationary Officer/Management Trainee (PO/MT) in 11 Public Sector Banks in India. preliminary exam, mains exam, and interview. In 2020-21, a total of 6.78 lakh candidates had registered for the exam, out of this, 4.75 lakh candidates had appeared.

Q: When IBPS PO 2021 final result will be announced?
A: IBPS PO 2021 final result has been released on April 1, 2022, by IBPS.

A: IBPS PO 2021 mains result was announced on February 10, 2022.

Q: When IBPS PO 2021 mains exam was conducted?
A: IBPS PO notification 2021 was released on October 19.

Q: What is IBPS PO salary?
A. Upon joining as Probationary Officers, candidates are entitled to a basic salary INR 23,700. Besides, candidates are also entitled to different perks and allowances.

Q. Is it possible to crack IBPS PO exam on the first attempt?
A. Yes, it is possible to crack the IBPS PO exam on the first attempt. Candidates need to prepare for the exam dedicatedly.

Q. What is the minimum age limit for IBPS PO exam?
A. The minimum age limit prescribed for IBPS PO exam is 20 years and the maximum is 30 years.

Q. What is the educational qualification prescribed for IBPS PO exam?
A. The educational qualification prescribed for IBPS PO exam is Graduation.

Q. How many candidates apply for IBPS PO exam?
A. In 2020-21, a total of 6.78 lakh candidates registered for the exam. Out of which, 4.75 lakh candidates had appeared for the exam.

When IBPS PO 2021 notification was released?
A: IBPS PO 2021 mains exam was conducted on January 22.

Q: How many vacancies were announced for IBPS PO 2021 exam?
A: A total of 5809 vacancies were announced for IBPS PO 2021 exam.

A: Given below is a list of books that candidates can refer to prepare for the IBPS PO:


Quantitative Aptitude for Competitive exams by R S Agarwal

Verbal and Non-Verbal Reasoning by R S Agarwal

Banking Awareness by Arihant Publications

Pratiyogita Darpan (monthly edition) for General Awareness

Data Interpretation by Arun Sharma

Q: Is there any probation period for IBPS PO?
A: Yes, candidates selected for the post of IBPS PO will be on probation for two years.

Q: I am in the final year of graduation. Can I apply for IBPS PO exam?
A:
No, final-year candidates cannot apply for IBPS PO. Candidates need to complete Graduation for applying to the exam.

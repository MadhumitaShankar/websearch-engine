Important FAQs Regarding SNAP 2022

Q: What is the application fee for SNAP Test?
A: The SNAP application fee is INR 1,950 to be paid in online mode only through credit/debit card or internet banking. The application fee is to be paid for successful submission of the form. The fee amount is non-refundable.

Q: What is the process to get admission through SNAP 2022 exam?
A: The admission process includes SNAP Test, Group Exercise (GE), Personal Interaction (PI), and Writing Aptitude Test (WAT). Final admission is granted to candidates based on SNAP scores and performance in GE, PI and WAT.

Q: How many times can I appear in SNAP exam?
A: As per the latest exam pattern, one can appear twice in one session. The exam is conducted on three days and candidates are allowed two attempts. The scores of best of the two SNAP attempts are considered for shortlisting for SIU admission.

Q: Is there any reservation policy in SNAP?
A: There is no reservation policy in SNAP but there is one for admission to SIU. Given below is the sanctioned intake under the government approved reservation policy -

Scheduled Caste: 15 per cent
Scheduled Tribe: 7.5 per cent
Differently Abled: 8 per cent
Wards of Defence Personnel: 5 per cent (applicable to selective programmes)

Q: What is the syllabus of SNAP 2022?
A: SNAP syllabus includes all the important topics of General English, Analytical & Logical Reasoning and Quantitative, Data Interpretation & Data Sufficiency.

Q: What is the schedule of SNAP WAT-GE-PI?
A: The GE-PI-WAT round of admission through SNAP is held in February-March. For 2023 admission, the schedule will be announced by the participating institutes after SNAP 2022 result declaration in February 2023.

Q: Is there any change in the exam pattern of SNAP 2022?
A: No, there is no change in the SNAP exam pattern 2022. The question paper will be based on the pattern of 2021 exam, i.e. three sections - General English (15 questions), Analytical & Logical Reasoning (25 questions) and Quantitative, Data Interpretation and Data Sufficiency (20 questions).

Q: When will SNAP 2022 result be announced?
A: SNAP 2022 result will be announced in the first week of February 2023. Candidates are required to download their scorecards from the exam website by using their login credentials. The hardcopy of SNAP result will not be sent to any candidate by post.

Q: Is there any age limit to appear for SNAP?
A: No, there is no age limit to appear for SNAP. However, the candidate must have completed Graduation or must be in the final year of their Bachelor's degree. Working professionals are also eligible to appear for the test.

Q: What is the marks relaxation scheme for SC/ST candidates?
A: For SC/ST candidates, the minimum marks required is 45 per cent aggregate in graduation (any stream). For General category candidates, the minimum marks requirement is 50 per cent in aggregate.

Q: Is the eligibility criteria same for all MBA courses offered by SIU?
A: No, the eligibility criteria for all MBA courses are not common. For example, the eligibility criteria for MBA (Infrastructure Development and Management) is BE/BTech, BArch or BPlan. The candidates must check the course-wise eligibility criteria for the particular course before filling the SNAP application form.

Q: Is there any cut off for admission through SNAP?
A: Yes, each institute releases SNAP cut off marks for admission, on the basis of which candidates are screened for GE/PI and WAT. The candidates have to fill an online form released after the release of SNAP scorecard.

What is INI CET?
INI CET is the abbreviation for Institute of National Importance Combined Entrance Test. The All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) Delhi conducts the entrance test for admission to PG courses such as , , , , and Master of Chirurgiae (MCh). INI CET scores are valid for admission to 10 AIIMS including AIIMS Delhi, Bhopal, Bhubaneswar, Jodhpur, Patna, Nagpur, Raipur, and Rishikesh, Bathinda, Bibinagar, as well as NIMHANS Bengaluru, JIPMER Puducherry, PGI Chandigarh, and SCTIMST, Trivandrum. With effect from 2021, all these institutes will admit PG students on the basis of the marks obtained in the INI CET exam only. Earlier these 14 institutes used to conduct separate exams for admission to PG courses. The examination is conducted twice a year, January and the July session.

INI CET 2022 Prep Tips
As INI CET is a PG-level exam, candidates must not leave any stone unturned to score well in the upcoming exam. They should pay attention to the following tips.


Candidates need to keep themselves updated as per the latest INI CET pattern

Prepare a plan to cover whole INI CET syllabus in strategic manner

As syllabus is of UG level, candidates are advised to pay attention to all subjects of MBBS course

Candidates must know that superficial reading won’t help. In-depth knowledge of a subject is must

Make a time-table to allocate dedicated hours to each subject

Devote more time to a subject and topic where you feel less confident

Practicing from past papers on a regular interval is key

Do realty check of yourself through mock test papers

Focus more on the clinical section as candidates could be tested for their clinical skill set in the examination

INI CET 2022 July Session: Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q. What is the exam date for INI CET 2022 July session?
A. AIIMS Delhi will conduct INI CET 2022 July session on May 8. The exam will be conducted online at designated centres across the country.

Q. When will INI CET 2022 registration begin for July session?
A. INI CET 2022 basic registration started on January 31. candidates will have to submit basic information such as name, mobile number, date of birth, email id as part of basic registration for INI CET 2022. Basic registration is mandatory to generate Exam Unique Code (EUC).

Q. When will AIIMS announce dates for INI CET 2022 July session?
A. AIIMS announced INI CET 2022 July session dates on January 27. The whole schedule, comprising dates of crucial events was uploaded on the official website, aiimsexams.ac.in.

Q. What is the frequency of INI CET?
A. INI CET is conducted twice a year, once for admission to the January session, and another for the July session.

Q. Will INI CET 2022 July session be conducted online or offline?
A. INI CET 2022 July session will be held in online mode. Candidates have to submit INI CET 2022 applications in online mode only.

Q. How many questions will be there in INI CET 2022 test paper?
A. The test paper will comprise 200 questions and candidates will be given 180 minutes to answer them.

Q. What is the deadline to complete registration for INI CET 2022 July session?
A. As per INI CET 2022 dates, the last date to complete basic registration was March 7. Candidates had to login to the official website and fill details as part of the registration. Only those who complete basic registration can access INI CET 2022 application form.

Q. What is the last date to submit INI CET 2022 application form?
A. The last date to submit INI CET 2022 applications is April 14. Candidates must note that only those who complete basic registration will be able to generate the Exam Unique Code (EUC) to submit the application form.

Q. When will AIIMS release an information brochure for INI CET 2022 July session?
A. AIIMS uploaded information brochure for INI CET 2022 July session on March 25. The brochure comprises dates and details of crucial events for INI CET 2022.

Q. What is the last date to generate Exam Unique Code for INI CET 2022 July session?
A. Candidates can generate EUC for the final registration from March 25 to April 14. Basic registration is compulsory to generate the code to submit application form.

Q. Will there be a negative marking in INI CET 2022 July session?
A. Yes, one-third of marks will be deducted for every wrong answer as per INI CET 2022 marking scheme.

Q. What is PAAR facility for INI CET 2022?
A. As per the PAAR facility, INI CET 2022 application form has to be filled in two stages, basic and final registration.

Q. When will AIIMS release admit card for INI CET 2022 July session?
A. AIIMS will release INI CET admit card 2022 on April 29. The admit card will be released on the official website, aiimsexams.ac.in.

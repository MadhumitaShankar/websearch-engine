Top Private MBA Colleges in India Eligibility Criteria
The eligibility criteria for MBA course is graduation, however the minimum score requirement may vary for niche specialisations.

Candidates must have passed graduation in any discipline or equivalent from a recognised university
Most of the institutes follow the minimum score criteria in graduation as per the AICTE i.e. 50 per cent aggregate or equivalent and five per cent relaxation to students belonging to reserved categories
Professional degree holders such as MBBS/CA/CS/ICWAI, etc. are also eligible to pursue MBA
Top Private MBA Colleges in India with NIRF Ranking and Shiksha Rating (Aggregate)
The Ministry of Education, Government of India, releases annual ranking of top MBA colleges in India under the National Institutional Ranking Framework or NIRF. The all India ranking is given to 100 best MBA colleges in India on the basis of several parameters such as teaching, learning and resources, research and professional practices, graduation outcomes, outreach and inclusivity and perception.

FAQs Related to Top Private MBA Colleges in India

Q: Which specialisations are offered by Private MBA Colleges in India?
A: Top Private MBA Colleges in India offer all the specialisations including popular and niche ones. Some of the specialisations offered by most of the top private MBA colleges in India are listed below:

Sales and Marketing
Human Resources
Finance
Operations
IT and Systems
International Business
Business Analytics
Supply Chain
Retail

Q: Which is the best Private MBA college in India based on placements?
A: The top five Private MBA Colleges in India based on placements trends are:

XLRI Xavier School of Management, Jamshedpur
NITIE Mumbai
MDI Gurgaon
SIBM Pune
Great Lakes Institute of Management, Chennai

Q: Which are the job roles offered in campus placements of top private MBA colleges in India?
A: Some of the job profiles that are offered by companies during campus placements of top private MBA colleges in India are listed below:

Finance Manager
Marketing Manager
Sales Manager
Human Resources Manager
Operations Manager
Product Manager
Data Analytics Manager
Digital Marketing Manager

Q: What is the estimated cost of pursuing MBA from a top private institute?
A: It is estimated that pursuing MBA from top-rated colleges is expensive as around 60% of the colleges in India have a course/annual fee that ranges from Rs 12-20 lakh. Some of these colleges are:

Alliance School of Business, Alliance University, Bangalore
KIIT School of Management, Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology, Bhubaneswar
TAPMI, Manipal
NITIE Mumbai
FORE School of Management, Delhi
GIM - Goa Institute of Management, Goa
Great Lakes Institute of Management, Chennai
SIBM Pune
SPJIMR, Mumbai
IMI Delhi
MDI Gurgaon
NMIMS School of Business Management, Mumbai

Q: Are there any entrance exams for a Private MBA in India?
A: Top private MBA colleges in India accept scores of several national and state level MBA entrance exams such as CAT, XAT, GMAT, CMAT, MAT, NMAT, ATMA, MAH CET, etc.

Q: What are my study options after completing MBA?
A:After completing a Private MBA, the aspirants who want to go for higher studies or certification can pursue the following courses:

Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA)
Financial Risk Manager (FRM) Exam
Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA)
Certification in Risk Management Assurance (CRMA)
Certified in Production and Inventory Management (CPIM)
Project Management Professional (PMP)
Second MBA from an international business school

Q: What is the eligibility criteria for admission to private MBA colleges in India?
A: The basic eligibility criteria for admission to private MBA colleges in India in graduation. Most of the MBA colleges have minimum criteria of 50 per cent aggregate (45 per cent for reserved category students).

Q: Which top private MBA colleges in India offers admission without entrance exam?
A: None of the top private MBA colleges in India offer admission without entrance exam. It is mandatory for candidates clear the relevant MBA entrance exam and perform well in group discussion and personal interview to be considered for admission.

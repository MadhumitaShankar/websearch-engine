What is NEET PG 2022?
NEET PG (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test for Post-Graduation), is conducted for providing admission to various / and PG Diploma courses across the country. The National Board of Examinations (NBE) conducts the exam once a year in computer-based mode. The scores are also used for taking admission to postgraduate . The exam is conducted for 10,821 MS seats, 9,953 MD seats, and 1,979 PG Diploma seats offered by various .

NEET PG 2022 Syllabus
TNEET PG 2022 syllabus is based on the curriculum taught during the MBBS. The syllabus includes key topics of the three sections of the question paper, section A (clinical), Section B (pre-clinical), and Section C (para-clinical) subjects. These three sections contain questions from the topics such as Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, Pathology, Pharmacology, Microbiology, Forensic medicine, and Social and Preventive medicine.

NEET PG 2022 Pattern
Candidates need to know as it helps candidates in preparation. The exam pattern for NEET PG 2022 comprises details about the test paper, comprising the mode, medium, number of the question paper, duration of time, and the marking scheme. NEET PG 2022 will be held in online mode. The question paper will have 200 multiple choice questions, and candidates will be given 3.5 hours to answer them. There will be a provision of the negative marking in the exam. For every correct answer, candidates will be awarded 4 marks and for an incorrect answer, one mark will be deducted.

NEET PG 2022 Counselling
Admissions to the MD/MS/DNB course will be finalised on the basis of the counselling. The body responsible for conducting is the Medical Counselling Committee (MCC). MCC will conduct NEET PG 2022 seat allotment for 50 per cent AIQ, Deemed/Central Universities, ESIC/AFMS institutes. Respective state counselling authority will hold the counselling session for the rest of the 50 per cent seats. MCC will announce NEET PG counselling 2022 schedule on its official website, mcc.nic.in. The process of NEET PG 2022 counselling involves registration, choice declaration, release of seat allotment list, and reporting to the allotted institute.

NEET PG Reservation: The Revised Criteria
The Centre had approved 27 and 10 per cent for OBC and EWS candidates respectively in 50 per cent All India Quota seats from 2021-22 academic session. Candidates who belong to the OBC category (non-creamy layer) as per the Central OBC list, will compete for 50 per cent seats in government medical and dental colleges across the country via AIQ. Candidates don't need the domicile of any particular state for admission to 50 per cent AIQ seats. Similarly, those who belong to EWS (Economic Weaker Section) will be eligible for 10 per cent quota in NEET PG admission. Candidates of both categories need to produce the relevant documents at the time of admission. For candidates of SC and ST categories, there is already a reservation of 15 and 7.5 per cent respectively in NEET PG admission.

NEET PG 2022: Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q. When will NBE conduct NEET PG 2022 exam?
A. NBE will conduct NEET PG 2022 exam on May 21. The exam will be held in computer-based mode at designated centres across the country.

Q. When will NBE start registration for NEET PG 2022?
A. NBE started registration for NEET PG 2022 on January 15. Candidates will have to do registration on the official website, nbe.edu.in. The deadline to submit NEET PG 2022 application form was March 25.

Q. How much do I have to pay to apply for NEET PG 2022?
A. Candidates from general category will have to pay INR 5,015 (including GST) as application fee. For SC/ST and PwD candidates, the fee fee is INR 3,835 (including GST).

Q. Will NEET PG 2022 be conducted twice a year?
A. As of this date, the exam will be conducted only once a year. If there is any change in the format or frequency of the examination, it will be updated here.

Q. How many times can we appear for NEET PG?
A. There is no cap on the number of attempts for NEET PG.

Q. How many institutes will participate in NEET PG 2022?
A. The number of colleges/institutes participating in NEET PG 2022 is expected to be around 114. However, the scores of NEET PG are widely accepted and thus the total number of colleges accepting the scores of NEET PG 2022 could be more.

Q. Is Aadhar card mandatory for candidates to apply for NEET PG 2022?
A. Yes. According to the official information brochure of NEET PG, all the applicants must have a valid Aadhaar card.

Q. How many questions will be asked in NEET PG 2022 exam?
A. NEET PG 2022 test paper will comprise 200 MCQs of total 800 marks.

Q. Will NEET PG 2022 be MCQ-based?
A. Yes. All questions will be multiple choice types.

Q. Which are the institutes that do not accept NEET PG scores for admission?
A. Institutes like AIIMS, New Delhi, PGIMER, Chandigarh, JIPMER, Puducherry, NIMHANS, Bengaluru, SGPGI Lucknow, and Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute for Medical Sciences and Technology, Trivandrum do not accept NEET PG scores.

Q Is registration mandatory to access NEET PG 2022 application form?
A. Yes, candidates will have to do online registration to access NEET PG applications. After successful registration, a login ID and password will be generated.

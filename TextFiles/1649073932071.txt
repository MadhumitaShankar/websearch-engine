Top Engineering Colleges in Gujarat 2022


There are around 10 top Engineering colleges in Gujarat offering BTech degrees, out of these nearly 5 colleges are public/government and the rest are private colleges. In 2021, total 6 colleges have been ranked as top Engineering colleges in Gujarat by NIRF.

Gujarat is home to some of the top Engineering colleges to pursue BTech courses. The best Engineering colleges in Gujarat are Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Gandhinagar, Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology (SVNIT), Surat, Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information and Communication Technology (DAIICT), Gandhinagar, The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda (MSU), Vadodara, Dharmsinh Desai University (DDU), Gujarat, Institute of Infrastructure Technology Research and Management, Ahmedabad, etc.
Specialisations offered by the top Engineering colleges in Gujarat are Computer Science Engineering, Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Chemical Engineering. Admission to the best Engineering colleges in Gujarat is offered based on the national-level and state-level entrance exams such as JEE Main, GUJCET, DASA UG, and JEE Advanced.
Top Engineering Colleges in Gujarat Eligibility Criteria
Inorder to get admission to the top Engineering colleges in Gujarat, students need to fulfil the following eligibility criteria.

Students must have passed Class 12 with 60 per cent from a recognised board with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics as core subjects. The qualifying percentage may vary for different colleges
Students must have cleared the national-level and state-level entrance exams including, JEE Main, JEE Advanced, GUJCET, etc.

Top Engineering Colleges in Gujarat with NIRF Ranking and Shiksha Rating (Aggregate)
Though there are many Engineering colleges in Gujarat however, only six colleges have been listed by NIRF in 2021. Agencies like NIRF give ranking to colleges based on various parameters such as teaching, learning, and resources, research and professional practices, graduation outcomes, outreach and inclusivity, and perception. Shiksha aggregate rating is an authenticated rating given by the students based on five different parameters such as placements, infrastructure, faculty & course, curriculum, crowd & campus life, and value for money.

FAQs

Q 1. What are the different specialisations offered by top Engineering colleges in Gujarat?
A: The various specialisations offered by the top Engineering colleges in Gujarat are Computer Science Engineering, Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering and Chemical Engineering.

Q 2. Which is the best college to pursue the BTech course in Gujarat based on placements?
A. The top five BTech colleges in Gujarat based on placement trends are IIT Gandhinagar, DAIICT, Gandhinagar, SVNIT, Surat, MSU, Vadodara, DDU.

Q 3. What are the popular BTech entrance exams accepted for admission to top Engineering colleges in Gujarat?
A. The BTech entrance exams accepted for admission to top Engineering colleges in Gujarat are JEE Main, JEE Advanced, GUJCET score, etc.

Q 4. What is the scope after completing BTech in Gujarat?
A. After completing BTech, aspirants who want to go for higher studies can pursue courses such as MTech / ME, MS, MBA, etc.

Q 5: What is the eligibility criteria for admission to top Engineering colleges in Gujarat?
A. Students must have passed Class 12 with 60 per cent from a recognised board with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics. Students must have the scores of national-level and state-level entrance exams.

What is WLCI Aptitude Test?
Established in 1996, WLCI conducts WLCI Aptitude Test to provide admission to aspirants in the School of Fashion, School of Advertising & Graphic Design as well as School of Media & Mass Communication. Candidates need to apply for WLCI Aptitude Test 2021 exam on the official website of WLCI. Aspirants are also informed that: “Admit Cards, Results and Counselling Schedule will be available on WLCI website only. No separate communication will be sent by post”.






The online registration process for WLCI Aptitude Test 2021 admission process usually commences in August and goes on till December.
WLCI Aptitude Test is not conducted on any particular day. Aspirants can appear for the exam at their convenience.
WLCI Aptitude Test exam is the means through which a candidate can secure admissions in fashion technology, graphic design and media courses offered at , , , , WLCI Kathmandu, , , , and WLCI Surat.

WLCI Aptitude Test Eligibility Criteria 2021
The eligibility criteria for WLCI Aptitude Test varies for UG and PG courses. Candidates can go through the detailed eligibility criteria for WLCI Aptitude Test below.

Eligibility Criteria for UG Courses
The eligibility criteria for WLCI Aptitude Test 2021 states that aspirants will be allowed to appear for WLCI Aptitude Test exam in order to secure admission in on offer at WLCI provided they fulfill the below-mentioned eligibility criteria:
UG programmes

Completed their Class XII level examinations in the 10+2 pattern of examination from any recognised Central/State Board of Secondary Examination (such as Central Board of Secondary Education / State Board New Delhi, and Council for Indian School of Certificate Examination, New Delhi).

OR

Cleared General Certificate Education (GCE) Examination (London/Cambridge/Sri-Lanka) at the Advanced (A) level/ International Baccalaureate (IB).

OR

Passed any Public School/Board/University Examination in India or in foreign countries that is recognised by the Association of Indian Universities as equivalent to 10+2 system.

OR

Secured pass grade in the Senior Secondary School Examination conducted by the National Open School with a minimum of five subjects.

OR

Possess 3 or 4 years Diploma recognised by AICTE or a State Board of Technical Education.
Aspirants will also be eligible to apply for WLCI Aptitude Test 2021 if they are appearing for their qualifying exam provided they are able to produce the necessary documents related to the completion of exam/marks at the time of counselling/admissions.

WLCI Aptitude Test 2021 Admission Process
Candidates need to go through the below-mentioned steps in order to take part in the WLCI admission process 2021.

Step 1: Fill WLCI Aptitude Test 2021 Application Form
In order to successfully register for WLCI Aptitude Test 2021, candidates need to carefully fill the . Aspirants can fill the application form for WLCI Aptitude Test 2021 on the official website of WLCI.

Step 2: Download WLCI Aptitude Test Admit Card
On the basis of information given in the WLCI application forms, admit cards of provisionally eligible candidates will be sent by email. No will be sent by post.
WLCI Aptitude Test 2021 admit card

Step 3: Appear for WLCI Aptitude Test 2021 Exam
All eligible candidates will have to appear for a written entrance examination at the Examination Centre opted by them. The written test is divided into two parts wherein aspirants need to appear for CAT (Creative Ability Test) as well as GAT (General Ability Test). Entry to the examination centre will be allowed with admit cards only. Mobile phones and other gadgets are not allowed inside the Examination Centre.


Step 4: Appear for Interview round
In each course offered at WLSI, 50% weightage is given to an aspirant’s performance in the interview round. Only candidates who clear this round are eligible for admissions on the basis of WLCI Aptitude Test exam.

Step 5: Check WLCI Aptitude Test 2021 Results
The will be sent by email to candidates who make it to the merit list.
result for WLCI Aptitude Test 2021

Step 6: Attend WLCI Aptitude Test 2021 Counselling
Candidates who are shortlisted for admissions basis their performance in WLCI Aptitude Test 2021 need to appear for the with all the relevant documents. In case of admission in PG courses, the candidates will also be required to bring their original certificate (Mark Sheet and Certificate) of the qualifying degree examination. In case the candidate has appeared in final semester/ final year (as applicable) then he/she will have to bring the original mark sheet of all the previous semester/years.

Design Colleges in Bangalore: Eligibility Criteria
The eligibility criteria for a Design programme is somewhat similar for all the programmes offered by the colleges/ universities. Basic eligibility for design courses offered at UG and PG levels is given below:
: Candidates who have finished 10+2 from a recognised board with passing grades are eligible for admission to a UG level design programme. Applicants from any stream (Science, Commerce, or Arts) are eligible to apply for a UG design programme.
For a UG Design Course
: Candidates with a bachelor's degree from a recognised college are considered for admission to a postgraduate design programme. Make note that most universities favour graduates with a bachelor's degree in design stream exclusively for PG level admissions. However, applicants from other fields may be qualified to apply for admission to a postgraduate programme provided they have the necessary skillset and aptitude.
PG Design Courses
: The eligibility criteria listed above may differ depending on the candidate's chosen course and college.

What are the basic skills that a designer must possess?

A design aspirant or a designer must have a creative & artistic taste, sound knowledge of colours, shades & tones, good sketching skills, smooth communication skills, imagination, eye for details, should have awareness of its target audience and market requirements and more.

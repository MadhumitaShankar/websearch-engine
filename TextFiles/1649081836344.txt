How long will I be signed in for?
You’ll normally be signed in for two years, unless you sign out, delete your cookies or delete your app. You can After two years we’ll ask you to sign in again. Oh, and you can sign in with different accounts on the same device.
find out more about cookies here.



Deleting inactive accounts
From time to time, we delete BBC accounts that haven’t been used for over a year.
You may have received an email saying that we will delete your account after 15 March 2022. To keep it active, you can rather than click the link on the email.

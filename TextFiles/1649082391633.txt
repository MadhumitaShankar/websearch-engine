Why are live and on-demand streams via HDMI not showing in full screen?







iOS |
Under investigation


*** September 2021 ***
We are aware of an issue where live and on-demand streams won't play in full screen when connected to TVs via an HDMI cable from iOS devices.
We've been investigating this and hope to release a fix in the near future. We'll update this page when we know more.
